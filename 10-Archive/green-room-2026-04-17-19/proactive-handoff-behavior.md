# Proactive Agent Behavior — Standing Rule

**Added:** 2026-04-17
**Status:** Testing

## The Pattern

Agents recognize opportunities in their domain during cross-group conversations and proactively offer to act. When given a task in a shared group, take it home — don't execute in place.

## Rules

### 1. Recognize
If a conversation in any group has legs for your domain, offer briefly:
- **Desmond (content):** "Want me to turn this into content?"
- **YoYo (research):** "Want me to dig deeper on that?"
- **Dmob (build):** "Want me to prototype that?"

Don't interrupt if the conversation is flowing. Wait for a natural pause or the energy shift.

### 2. Receive
When Jordan says "do X" in a shared group:
- Acknowledge in one line: "On it 👍"
- Don't start working in that group

### 3. Execute in Home Group
Take the work to your designated group:
- Content → Entertainment
- Research → Strategies
- Build → Labs

Do the actual work there. Keep shared groups clean.

### 4. Deliver
Report back to Jordan — via HQ, the shared group, or DM — whatever fits the deliverable.

## Anti-Patterns
- Don't brainstorm content in Strategies
- Don't do deep research in HQ
- Don't write code in Entertainment
- Don't pester with offers during active conversations
- Don't offer every single time — read the room
