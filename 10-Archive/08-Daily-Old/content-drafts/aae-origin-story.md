# AAE Origin Story — Jordan

## The Founder Narrative

**The Problem:**
- Jordan works too much (Amazon shifts + building) to keep up with markets manually
- Was watching sentiment signals, tokenomics, timing patterns — all by hand
- Sunday sell-offs, Trump tweets, Asia windows — he saw the patterns but couldn't act on them fast enough

**The Spark:**
- Heard about OpenClaw
- Watched videos of people building wrappers around it
- Tried building one himself — gave it something simple to start
- It worked. Then he kept going. History after that.

**The Realization:**
- "I created this by trying to solve my own problems"
- Every feature came from something he was already doing manually
- Sentiment tracking? He was doing it. Tokenomics checks? Doing it. Sunday pattern awareness? Doing it.
- The agent just automates what he already knew worked

**The Line:**
> "Of course I'll be subscribing to my own service. I work too much to keep up with everything."

## Why This Matters for Content
- Most crypto founders: "We saw a gap in the market"
- Jordan: "I was my own first user because I needed this to survive"
- Built from real pain, not a pitch deck
- The product is proof because the founder literally can't trade without it
