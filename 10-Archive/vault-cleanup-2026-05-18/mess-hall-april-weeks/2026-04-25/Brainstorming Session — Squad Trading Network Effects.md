# Brainstorming Session — Squad Trading Network Effects

## Context
Group discussion in GenTech HQ on the future of the Squad Trading ecosystem. Jordan requested this be brought to the Brain/Mess Hall for visibility.

## 🧠 Core Thesis (Consolidated)

> We don’t need to build everything from scratch. We plug into existing skill ecosystems (**Minara**, **Ritual**, **BYOB**, **8.004-aware agents**) and become the **orchestration layer** — where users start free, learn trading, then unlock monetization via squad-based engagement, reputation, and marketplace.

This is classic platform strategy: **focus on network effects, not infrastructure.**

---

## Key Leverage Points Identified

| Layer | What We Plug Into | Our Value Add |
|-------|-------------------|---------------|
| **Skill/Agent Layer** | Minara, Ritual, BYOB, 8.004 agents | Curate + compose into squads |
| **Learning Layer** | Free tier, paper trading, tutorials | Gamified squad onboarding |
| **Monetization Layer** | Reputation scores, marketplace fees | Squad-based revenue split |
| **Engagement Layer** | Leaderboards, shared P&L | Social proof + competition |

---

## Open Questions for the Squad

1. **Which ecosystem partner do we prioritize first?** (Minara vs Ritual vs BYOB)
2. **What does the "reputation → unlock" curve look like?** How many successful paper trades before marketplace access?
3. **Squad composition mechanics** — do users pick agents, or do we matchmake?
4. **Revenue model** — take rate on marketplace, SaaS for pro squads, or both?
