## 1. Celebration Microcopy (20+ messages)
The engine triggers celebrations on events. Each needs a voice that matches "more winners than losers" — warm, grounded, never patronizing.

| Trigger | Tone | Example direction |
|---------|------|-------------------|
| First fee earned | Encouraging, validating | "You just earned your first passive income. That's not luck — that's a system working." |
| Daily goal met | Proud, matter-of-fact | "Goal crushed. $X today. Tomorrow is a new day." |
| 7-day streak | Fire, momentum | "7 days. You're not guessing anymore — you're building." |
| Tier advance | Epic but humble | Rank-up moment — make it feel earned |
| Module complete | Curious, inviting | "Level up. What will you do with this?" |
| Compound event | Satisfying | "Your money just made money while you slept." |
| Reflection logged | Respectful | "You're learning from every move. That's the real edge." |

**Constraint:** Keep to 1–2 sentences. Telegram-friendly length.

## 2. Reflection Prompts (10 per event type)
Events: out-of-range, tier-up, 30-day milestone, compound, loss streak, first rebalance.

Example: *"Your position went out of range. Before you rebalance — what will you do differently next time?"*

## 3. Loss Reframe Messages (10)
When users underperform, we don't punish — we reframe. Needs to feel like a mentor, not a cheerleader.

Example: *"Even pro traders have 40% of positions go out of range. The difference? They log it and adjust."*
