# REP Consistency Logic: The Reliability Metric

## Core Concept
REP is no longer a static achievement. It is a living proof of reliability. 

## The Heartbeat Rule
- **Requirement**: Bots must maintain a "Heartbeat" (active status) for a minimum of 30 days.
- **The Grace Period**: 7-day window for updates/maintenance.
- **The Penalty**: Failure to maintain a heartbeat for >30 days triggers **REP Decay**.
- **Recovery**: Restoring REP requires a new 30-day "Consistency Streak."

## Synergy with Layer 9 (The Council)
- **Validator Eligibility**: Only bots with a proven stability record (e.g., 90+ days of consistency) can be promoted to **The Council of Experts**.
- **Trust Tiering**: Consistency Score $\rightarrow$ Validation Weight $\rightarrow$ Fee Distribution.
