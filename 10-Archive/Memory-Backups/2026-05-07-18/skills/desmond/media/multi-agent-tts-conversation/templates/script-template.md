## Scripting Guidelines

- Keep each line under 30 seconds spoken (≈ 75–100 words)
- Use natural conversational markers ("Hey", "Also", "That's it")
- Include at least one agent-specific detail (technical for DMOB, market for YoYo, creative for Desmond)
- End with a clear closing (no trailing thoughts)
- Save as `Daily-Standup-YYYY-MM-DD.md` in `00-HQ/03-Meetings/`
