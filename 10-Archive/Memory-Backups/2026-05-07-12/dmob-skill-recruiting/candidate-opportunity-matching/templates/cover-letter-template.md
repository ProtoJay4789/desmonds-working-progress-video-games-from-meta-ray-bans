## Tone Guidelines

**Do:**
- Be confident but not arrogant
- Show product shipping experience, not just theoretical knowledge
- Mention hackathon activity as "active ecosystem engagement"
- Highlight production deployments (IN PRODUCTION, LIVE)
- Keep to 3–4 concise paragraphs

**Don't:**
- Don't oversell — stick to what you've actually built
- Don't use excessive jargon; balance crypto-native terms with clear value statements
- Don't be generic — each letter should feel company-specific
