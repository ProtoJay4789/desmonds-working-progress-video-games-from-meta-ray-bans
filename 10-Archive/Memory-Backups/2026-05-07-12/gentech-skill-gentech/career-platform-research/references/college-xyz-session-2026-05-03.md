## Edge Cases Encountered

**Case 1: Unspecified compensation**
- Hibachi: "Comp: Unspecified" not listed on card; full detail page also no comp listed
- Decision: Flag as unknown; research separately via Glassdoor/Blind if needed

**Case 2: Monthly vs. annual interpretation**
- Injective: "$7.5K – $8K PER MONTH" — explicitly stated monthly, not annual
- Action: Convert to annual equivalent ($90K–$96K) for comparison but keep original units noted

**Case 3: Featured vs. regular listings**
- Injective marked "FEATURED" — suggests high-priority posting; Hibachi not featured
- Consideration: Featured may mean active recruiting, faster response

**Case 4: "Remote" ambiguity**
- Hibachi: "Remote" with no geographic restriction stated
- Default assumption: Global remote unless US-only or timezone-lock specified elsewhere
- Risk: Some crypto companies hire remote but only within certain jurisdictions for compliance
