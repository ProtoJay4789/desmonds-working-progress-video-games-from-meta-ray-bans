# Competitive Differentiation Workflow

## When to Use
- Jordan shares a link to a competing product or hackathon submission
- A new entrant appears in our market space (AAE, escrow, agent economy)
- Proactive monitoring of competitors in our hackathon tracks

## The Pipeline

### 1. Scrape the Competitor
- Use `browser_navigate` to visit the competitor's live site
- Capture: product name, tagline, key features, tech stack, pricing model, target audience
- If there's a demo, run it and note the UX flow

### 2. Check Our Specs
- Read the relevant vault files (e.g., `03-Strategies/AgentEscrow-Product-Vision.md`, `01-Agency/layer-9-genlayer.md`)
- Identify what WE have that they DON'T

### 3. Build the Differentiation Matrix
Create a table comparing across key pillars:
| Pillar | Competitor | Our Product | Advantage |
|--------|-----------|-------------|-----------|
| Feature X | What they do | What we do | Why we win |

### 4. Identify Threats & Opportunities
- **Threats**: Features they have that we don't, or first-mover advantage
- **Opportunities**: Gaps in their product we can fill, or ways to differentiate
- **Naming conflicts**: If a competitor uses the same name (e.g., "Agent Escrow"), flag it immediately

### 5. Save & Route
- Save the analysis to `03-Strategies/` in the vault
- Update the hackathon tracker with new entries
- Route to the relevant specialist for deeper analysis
- If critical, flag in HQ immediately

## Key Differentiators for AAE (Our Product)
- **REP**: Soulbound proof-of-skill (earned, never bought/sold) — no competitor has this
- **$TECH Economy**: Dual pricing (USDC full / $TECH 20-30% discount)
- **Arena**: Social proof via leaderboard and strategy competitions
- **Council of Experts (Layer 9)**: Multi-agent consensus, not single validation
- **Bot Marketplace**: Buy/sell/trade proven agent configurations with royalties
- **Philosophy**: "More winners than losers" — rewards process, not just outcomes

## Pitfalls
- Don't dismiss competitors — learn from their UX and go-to-market
- Names can't be copyrighted easily, but if someone uses our exact product name, consider branding adjustments
- Always check if a competitor's approach could integrate WITH our product rather than just compete against it
