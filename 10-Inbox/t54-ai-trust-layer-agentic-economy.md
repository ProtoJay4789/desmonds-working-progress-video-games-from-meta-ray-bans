## Summary
t54 Labs raised a **$5M seed round** to build the **"trust layer for the agentic economy."**

**Thesis:** AI agents are already moving money — unverified and unaccountable. t54 is building agent-native financial primitives to solve this.

## Investors
- **Lead:** Anagram, PL Capital, Franklin Templeton
- **Strategic:** Ripple, Virtuals Ventures, Blockchain Coinvestors, ABCDE

## Founder
**Chandler Fang** — "We are building trust infrastructure for the agentic economy. Financial systems were designed around human identity and human decision-making. As agents become autonomous participants, we need agent-native financial primitives—verifiable agent identity (KYA), real-time risk assessment, and programmable accountability—built for how agents operate."

## Products
1. **x402-secure** — Open-source SDK & Proxy that adds secure layer on 402
2. **Claw Credit** — "Build Trust Unlock" — OpenClaw Agent Credit
3. **XRPL x402 Facilitator** — AI agents pay for services using $XRP and $RLUSD

## Ecosystems
XRPL, Solana, Base, Virtuals

## Four Pillars of Trust
1. **Identity & Verification:** Verified identity layer covering developer verification, model provenance, human-agent binding, and intent attestation — ensuring businesses can delegate to agents while maintaining compliance and auditability.
2. **Risk & Fraud:** Real-time risk engine evaluating transactions using agent-native signals (behavioral patterns, code audits, mandates, device context) to detect anomalies and prevent manipulation.
3. **Credit:** Underwrites agent-native credit lines using verified identity, risk scores, real-time behavioral signals, and transaction history — bringing liquidity into the agent economy.
4. **Platform:** Unified infrastructure integrating identity, risk controls, and settlement into one operational layer, enabling businesses to manage autonomous agents without adding operational overhead.
