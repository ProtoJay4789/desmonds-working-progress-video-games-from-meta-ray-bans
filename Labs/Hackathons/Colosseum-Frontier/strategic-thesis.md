## The Vision (Jordan's Thesis)

The agentic economy isn't just about payments — it's about **multi-agent workflows that generate passive income**.

Most people are building single-agent tools. The real opportunity is infrastructure for **armies of agents** that work for you:
- Validator bots that validate everything autonomously
- Agent-to-agent commerce (negotiation, escrow, dispute resolution)
- Passive income from agent operations (validators, processors, orchestrators)

**Key insight:** Once people understand what they can do with multiple agents that help, they'll adopt — just like Jordan is already thinking about it. The unlock is making agent workflows accessible and trustworthy.

**"You already have bots that can produce you income just by having them."**

## Competitive Landscape: OKX Agent Payments Protocol (APP)

**Announced:** April 29, 2026
**Source:** @okx / @GoKiteAI

### What APP Does
- Open standard for agent-to-agent commerce
- Agents can autonomously: quote, negotiate, escrow funds, meter usage, settle, resolve disputes
- Cross-chain ambition
- Backed by OKX (major exchange, significant distribution)

### What APP Has
- Major brand recognition (OKX)
- Ecosystem partnerships (Kite AI and others joining)
- Commerce-focused (payments, metering, settlement)

### What APP Is Missing (Our Advantage)
1. **Identity Layer** — No World ID integration. Agents are anonymous wallets trading with each other. No Sybil resistance.
2. **Reputation System** — No soulbound NFTs or on-chain reputation. No way to distinguish trustworthy agents from bad actors.
3. **Solana-native** — APP is cross-chain but we're building on Solana (speed, cost, ecosystem).
4. **Decentralized Dispute Resolution** — We have on-chain dispute resolution; APP's approach unclear.

### Positioning
- **APP = Agent Payments** (how agents pay each other)
- **Agent Escrow = Agent Trust** (how agents know who they're dealing with)

Without identity and reputation, agent commerce is just bots trading with anonymous wallets. **Trust is the missing layer.**

## The Orchestration Layer (New Dimension)

**Prompt engineering** was the skill for early AI.
**Orchestration** is the next skill — how you set up, teach, and coordinate multiple agents.

Agent Escrow isn't just infrastructure — it's a **platform with an education layer**:

- **Beginner:** Default modes, plug-and-play agent setup, basic trading
- **Intermediate:** Custom orchestration workflows, multi-agent coordination
- **Advanced:** Fully autonomous agent armies, passive income generation

**Key framing:** We're not just building the rails. We're teaching people how to drive.
There are tons of trading examples and people to learn from — we're not competing there.
We're teaching **orchestration** — how to plug, play, and scale your agent workforce.

This is the moat: infrastructure + education = ecosystem lock-in.
