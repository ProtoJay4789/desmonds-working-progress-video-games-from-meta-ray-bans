## What It Is
Apollo is a layer that enables agents to autonomously handle money on top of BNB Chain and GenLayer.

## Core Thesis
> "Agents cannot open bank accounts, sign contracts, or file claims if they are defrauded. Blockchain is not a design choice—it is the only way where an agent can truly autonomously handle money."
