# AAE Portfolio Intelligence Layer — Jordan's Vision (Apr 21, 2026)

**Source:** Jordan voice note in HQ, responding to YoYo's LP tracker analysis
**Status:** Concept — save for AAE product build

## Core Concept

The AAE "body" should be a **portfolio intelligence layer** that gives users everything they need to know about their DeFi positions — without them having to go figure it out manually.

## Key Features (Jordan's List)

### 1. 💰 Position Tracking
- What you put in (deposits, LP positions, DCA amounts)
- What you're making (fees, rewards, yield)
- Real-time position status (in-range, out-of-range, efficiency)

### 2. 📈 Yield Farming Intelligence
- Daily earnings projection
- APR/APY tracking with sustainability analysis
- Fee efficiency monitoring
- Compound interest modeling

### 3. 🏆 Milestones & Goals
- Progress tracking toward targets ($5/day, $20/day, etc.)
- Timeline projections based on current trajectory
- Achievement unlocks

### 4. 🎓 Simulated Learning
- Interactive learning tied to actual portfolio performance
- Teach DeFi concepts through the user's real positions
- Skill progression tracking

## Business Model Options
- **Subscription tier:** Monthly access to portfolio intelligence
- **Pay-to-launch:** One-time fee for full feature access

## Connection to Trade Off Platform
This complements the ghost trading (Trade Off) product:
- Trade Off = skill-based ghost trading + engagement rewards
- Portfolio Intelligence = the "body" that shows real yield farming outcomes
- Together: learn by simulating → earn by yield farming → track everything in one place

Based on Jordan's own LP tracker that showed:
