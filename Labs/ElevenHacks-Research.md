## Submission Guide Insights

**Judging Philosophy:**
- "The best demos solve a real need and are genuinely entertaining"
- **Video is 50%+ of the judging** — "A brilliant project with a bad video loses to a decent project with a great video"
- 60-90 second videos, 5-second hook is critical
- Combine tools creatively — "The weirder the combination, the more memorable the demo"
- Build something real, not a toy demo

**Virality Formula:**
1. Solve a real pain point ("I need this" reaction)
2. Be extremely visual (show real-world use, not just terminal)
3. Surprise people ("wait, that's possible?" moment)
4. Ride the moment (trending topics, memes, cultural events)

## Opportunity for AAE

**Jordan's idea:** "Add agents who can speak well and don't drown you in text"

**Potential project concepts:**
1. **Voice-First Agent Interface** — Users interact with AI agents purely through voice. Agents speak concisely, no text walls. Perfect for ElevenLabs Voice Design/Cloning.
2. **Concise Agent Communication Protocol** — Agents that summarize complex DeFi/contract data into 30-second voice briefings.
3. **Multi-Agent Voice Debate** — Multiple agents with different personalities discuss a topic, users listen to the debate rather than reading.
4. **Voice-Powered Agent Marketplace** — Browse, evaluate, and hire agents through voice commands.

**Strategic fit:**
- ElevenLabs integration aligns with our TTS needs
- Free API credits reduce our costs
- Multiple hackathon entries possible across the 11 weeks
- Builds our voice agent capabilities for the social layer
