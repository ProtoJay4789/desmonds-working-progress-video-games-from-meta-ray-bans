⚡ Jordan — Lightning Invoker · Skill Synergy Map

🔄 Synergy Chain

Tempest Flurry → Shocks enemies → Falling Thunder → Burst on shocked → Thunderstorm → AoE shock chain → Herald of Thunder → Invoker scales
Mana Loop: Mana Drain + Mana Remnants sustain mana for continuous spellcasting.
Defense: Parry → counterattack pairs with Killing Palm for culls on low-health adds.

🎯 Recommended Upgrades

  • 1. Thunderstorm → Shock Conduction II + Overcharge
    100% shock chain across enemies. Cornerstone combo.
  • 2. Orb of Storms → Chain + Increased AoE
    Chain bounces bolts between enemies. AoE expands orb radius.
  • 3. Tempest Flurry → Shock Conduction II + Lightning Attunement
    Better shock coverage + physical→lightning conversion.
  • 4. Falling Thunder → Overcharge
    Boosts shock magnitude from Falling Thunder hits.

🔥 Vanito — Vibrant Warrior · Skill Synergy Map

🔄 Synergy Loop

Infernal Cry → Mace Strike / Rolling Slam → Bone Shatter → Wing Blast → Savage Fury → Rolling Magma / Thunderstorm
Physical Core: Infernal Cry warcry buffs your heavy hits. Mace Strike builds Rage, Rolling Slam uses it for massive AoE.
Elemental Overflow: Wing Blast generates charges → Savage Fury sustains buffs → Thunderstorm/The Vower/Rolling Magma for AoE elemental clear.
Cross-Set: Wing Blast charges feed into Infernal Cry buffs. Thunderstorm bridges both sets for hybrid clear.

🎯 Recommended Upgrades

  • 1. Bone Shatter → Stun + Brutality
    Stun amplifies its core mechanic; Brutality adds pure physical since you're not scaling elemental on Warrior.
  • 2. Mace Strike → Heavy Swing + Rage
    Heavy Swing adds massive more damage to your primary melee hit.
  • 3. Rolling Magma → Ignite Proliferation + Magnified Area
    Ignite spreads to nearby enemies — turns every hit into AoE clear.
  • 4. Thunderstorm → Lightning Penetration + Magnified Area
    Without penetration, lightning damage gets resisted hard in later acts.
  • 5. Raise Shield → Vanguard + Molten Shell
    Molten Shell adds a damage-absorbing shield on top of your block.
