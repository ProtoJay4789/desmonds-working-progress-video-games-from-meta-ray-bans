# Jordan's Monk Build

## Strategy
- **Role:** High-burst caster (Lightning/Ice) with mobility.
- **Playstyle:** "Melt" opponents while maintaining mana efficiency.
- **Ascendancy:** Invoker (keeping — best for lightning builds)

## Active Skills
- **Quarterstaff Strike:** Primary mobility and "On Hit" trigger.
- **Falling Thunder:** Massive AoE lightning damage for screen clearing.
- **Tempest Flurry:** Sustained lightning damage and movement speed buff. Quarterstaff strikes with electricity.
- **Orb of Storms:** Trapped lightning damage.
  - *Support:* **Slow Potency** (Keeps enemies in the kill zone).
- **Thunderstorm:** Massive AoE lightning damage (swapped from Ice Nova).
- **Mana Drain:** Core sustain skill to refill mana for heavy casts.
- **Frozen Locus:** Secondary freeze/damage source.
- **Siphoning Strike:** Life/Mana leech on hit for sustain.

## Synergy Loop
1. **Mobility:** Use Quarterstaff Strike to engage.
2. **Clear:** Drop Falling Thunder to break groups.
3. **Sustain:** Cast Mana Drain to refill resources instantly.
4. **Burst:** Cast **Thunderstorm** for massive AoE lightning damage, synergizing with **Orb of Storms** for "electrocute and melt" damage.
4. **Sustain:** Use **Siphoning Strike** to leech life/mana on hit during combat.

## Goals
- Optimize the link between Orb of Storms and Thunderstorm.
- Maintain high mana efficiency to prevent downtime.
- Use Siphoning Strike for sustain during extended fights.

## Patch 0.5 — Return of the Ancients (Live May 29, 2026)

### What Changed
- **New Monk Ascendancy: Martial Artist** — three Hollow Techniques (exclusive skills)
  - **Hollow Palm Technique** — unarmed attacks with quarterstaff skills, flat phys damage from skill level, attack speed from evasion, crit from energy shield
  - **Hollow Form** — channel to summon temporary illusions that perform socketed attacks
  - **Way of the Stonefist** — gloves transform to Fists of Stone with enhanced modifiers
  - **Martial Adept** — gain extra Combo, reduced ES recharge delay per Combo
- **Falling Thunder** — still viable. Popular 0.5 builds combine Falling Thunder + Tempest Flurry + Martial Artist ascendancy
- **Atlas Passive Tree** — fully reworked. New endgame structure.
- **Runes of Aldur** — new challenge league. Hub area "The Ruins of Kingsmarch" on Atlas.
- **Endgame content** — partially or fully reworked across the board

### Impact on Jordan's Build
- **Falling Thunder** remains strong — no nerfs spotted. Martial Artist ascendancy adds new combo synergies.
- **Orb of Storms + Thunderstorm** combo still viable.
- **Decision point:** Stay Invoker or respec to Martial Artist? Martial Artist's Hollow Palm + Combo mechanics could enhance the Quarterstaff Strike → Falling Thunder loop.
- **Tempest Frenzy** may need review — Tempest Flurry is the preferred pairing with Martial Artist in 0.5 builds.
