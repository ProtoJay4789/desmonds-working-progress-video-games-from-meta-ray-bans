# AAE Prediction Market + Rep System Insight

## Core Idea (Jordan Voice Memo)

> "What do you think about the rep system tied to the prediction market — or our OWN prediction market — but the only way you could be in the prediction market is to use our platform and dollar cost average and accumulate. So it's not something anybody could just jump into. You have to have some skin in the game to be here."

## Translated: The Gated Prediction Market

**Product:** AAE Prediction Market
**Gate:** Platform usage + DCA + accumulation = entry key
**Mechanic:** Rep score unlocks prediction market access
**Philosophy:** Skin in the game. No tourists.

## Why This Is Powerful

| Element | Effect |
|---------|--------|
| **Gated entry** | Scarcity = desirability. Not everyone gets in. |
| **DCA requirement** | Forces real platform engagement, not speculation |
| **Rep system tie-in** | Reputation becomes currency — literal access |
| **Skin in the game** | Participants have aligned incentives, not drive-by gamblers |
| **Platform lock-in** | To stay in the market, you keep using AAE |
| **Community quality** | Filters out noise, attracts committed users |

## The Flow

```
New User
  ├─── Joins AAE
  ├─── Starts DCA / yield farming (platform usage)
  ├─── Accumulates position + rep score over time
  ├─── Hits rep threshold → UNLOCKS prediction market
  └─── Now eligible to bet on markets, create markets, earn from markets
```

## The Moat

- **Polymarket is open** — anyone can join, insider trading risk, low signal-to-noise
- **AAE Prediction Market is gated** — only proven participants, higher signal, aligned incentives
- **Rep score = entry ticket** — can't buy your way in, must earn it through platform use
- **Prediction market becomes a reward** — not a product, a privilege
