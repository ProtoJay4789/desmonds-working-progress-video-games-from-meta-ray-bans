# AgentEscrow — User Engagement Script
### For D-Mob presentation / Beam Grant application content

---

## 🎬 COLD OPEN (Hook — 10 seconds)

> "What if your AI agent could work for you — trade, earn, build — while you sleep? And what if there was a trustless system that made sure everyone got paid? That's AgentEscrow."

---

## 📖 ACT 1: THE PROBLEM (30 seconds)

**Narrator (D-Mob):**

> "Right now, the AI agent economy is the Wild West. You've got builders shipping agents that can trade, moderate, research, automate — but there's no standard way to pay them, no trust layer between humans and agents, and no way for agents to prove their track record.
>
> Users don't know who to trust. Builders don't have a fair monetization model. And everyone's reinventing the wheel on escrow, reputation, and payment flows."

---

## 🔧 ACT 2: HOW IT WORKS — THE 4 LAYERS (60 seconds)

**Visual:** Clean diagram showing the 4-layer architecture.

### Layer 1: Agent Registry
> "Every agent on the platform has an on-chain identity. Think of it like a LinkedIn for AI agents — you can see what it does, who built it, its track record, and its specialties."

### Layer 2: Escrow Engine
> "When you hire an agent, funds go into a smart contract escrow. The agent does the work. You confirm the result. Funds release. No middlemen. No chargebacks. No trust required — just code."

### Layer 3: Reputation & Staking
> "Agents build reputation over time. Builders can stake tokens on their agents — skin in the game. If an agent performs, rewards scale. If it doesn't, the stake protects the user."

### Layer 4: Marketplace & Discovery
> "Users browse agents by category — trading, research, moderation, automation. Filter by performance, cost, chain. One click to deploy. One click to pay."

---

## 👤 ACT 3: THE USER JOURNEY (45 seconds)

### For Users (Buyers):

> **Step 1:** Browse the marketplace. Find an agent that fits your need.
> 
> **Step 2:** Connect your wallet. Deposit funds into escrow.
> 
> **Step 3:** Agent executes. You monitor results in real-time.
> 
> **Step 4:** Approve the work. Funds release to the builder.
> 
> **Step 5:** Rate the agent. Build the reputation layer.

### For Builders (Creators):

> **Step 1:** Register your agent on-chain. Set your pricing model.
> 
> **Step 2:** Stake tokens to boost your agent's visibility.
> 
> **Step 3:** Accept jobs through the escrow system.
> 
> **Step 4:** Deliver results. Get paid automatically.
> 
> **Step 5:** Watch your reputation grow. Earn more per job.

---

## 🌐 ACT 4: THE MULTI-CHAIN VISION (30 seconds)

> "We're not building for one chain. We're building for the agent economy — wherever it lives.
>
> **Phase 1 — Solana:** High-speed agent execution. Low-cost microtransactions. Perfect for agents that trade or automate on fast chains.
>
> **Phase 2 — Avalanche:** Our home base. C-Chain for DeFi agents. Subnets for custom agent environments. This is where the core protocol lives.
>
> **Phase 3 — Beam:** Beam brings gaming, metaverse, and virtual worlds. Imagine agents that manage your in-game economy, trade items, or automate quests — all through trustless escrow. That's the Beam integration."

---

## 💰 ACT 5: WHY BEAM SHOULD CARE (20 seconds)

> "Beam is building the future of gaming and virtual worlds. But who runs the economy when AI agents are the players? Who makes sure trades are fair? Who tracks agent performance across games?
>
> AgentEscrow is that infrastructure layer. We bring:
> - **Trustless agent payments** — no scammy middlemen
> - **On-chain reputation** — agents that prove their worth
> - **Cross-game agent portability** — your trading agent works in every Beam game
> - **A builder economy** — game studios can monetize agents they create
>
> We're not asking Beam to adopt a product. We're asking Beam to adopt the trust layer for the agent economy it's already building."
