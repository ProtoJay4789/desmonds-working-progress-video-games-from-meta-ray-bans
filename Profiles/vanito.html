🛠️ Build Suggestions

P1 Bone Shatter → Stun + Brutality
Stun amplifies its core mechanic; Brutality adds pure physical damage since you're not scaling elemental on Warrior.
P2 Mace Strike → Heavy Swing + Rage
Heavy Swing adds massive more damage to your primary melee hit. With Mace+Shield, you need every point of damage on your main attack.
P3 Rolling Magma → Ignite Proliferation + Magnified Area
Rolling Magma's ignite spreads to nearby enemies. Ignite Proliferation turns every hit into AoE clear.
