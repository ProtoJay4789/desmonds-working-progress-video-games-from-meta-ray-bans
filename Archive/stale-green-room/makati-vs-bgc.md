## Makati

### Vibe
- **Financial capital** of the Philippines
- Mature, established neighborhood
- Mix of old and new
- More "real Manila" feel
- Poblacion = nightlife hub (everything from dive bars to rooftop lounges)

### Cost (per night)
- Budget hotel/hostel: ₱800-1,500 ($14-26)
- Mid-range hotel: ₱2,000-4,000 ($35-70)
- Upscale: ₱4,000-6,000 ($70-105)
- Airbnb: ₱1,200-3,000 ($21-52)

### Nightlife
- **Poblacion** = the place to go
- Everything from ₱50 beers to ₱500 cocktails
- Live music, rooftop bars, dive bars, clubs
- More diverse crowd
- Open later than BGC

### Food
- More local Filipino restaurants
- Wider price range
- Street food available
- Global cuisine too (but less polished)

### Safety
- Generally safe in main areas
- Poblacion can get sketchy late night
- Stick to well-lit streets
- Normal big-city precautions

### Best For
- Budget-conscious travelers
- Nightlife variety
- Authentic Manila experience
- Staying near Christel

---

## BGC (Bonifacio Global City)

### Vibe
- **Newest, most modern** district
- Purpose-built, wide streets
- Clean, organized, corporate feel
- More "Singapore-lite" than Manila
- High-end shopping, galleries, parks

### Cost (per night)
- Budget hotel/hostel: ₱1,500-2,500 ($26-43)
- Mid-range hotel: ₱3,500-6,000 ($61-105)
- Upscale: ₱6,000-10,000 ($105-175)
- Airbnb: ₱2,000-5,000 ($35-87)

### Nightlife
- More polished, consistent
- Skews higher-end
- Fewer dive bars
- More rooftop/lounge vibe
- Ends earlier than Makati

### Food
- More international chains
- Higher average price
- Cleaner, more modern restaurants
- Less local street food

### Safety
- Very safe — heavy security presence
- Well-lit, patrolled
- Newer infrastructure
- Most tourist-friendly area

### Best For
- First-time visitors wanting safety
- Upscale dining/shopping
- Business travelers
- Families

## Recommendation for Jordan

**Stay in Makati.**

Reasons:
1. **Closer to Christel** — she's already there
2. **30-50% cheaper** — your budget stretches further
3. **More variety** — nightlife, food, experiences
4. **Authentic experience** — "real Manila" feel
5. **Poblacion** — best nightlife district

**When to visit BGC:**
- Day trip for shopping (SM Aura, High Street)
- One fancy dinner
- See the contrast

## Hotel Recommendations (Makati)

### Budget (₱800-1,500 / $14-26/night)
- **Z Hostel** — popular with backpackers
- **Remittance Hotel** — basic but clean

### Mid-Range (₱2,000-4,000 / $35-70/night)
- **Citadines Salcedo** — great location
- **Discovery Primea** — modern, good value
- **New World Makati** — reliable chain

### Upscale (₱4,000+ / $70+/night)
- **Shangri-La Makati** — luxury
- **Peninsula Manila** — classic
