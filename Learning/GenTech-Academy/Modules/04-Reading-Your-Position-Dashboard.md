# Module 4: Reading Your Position Dashboard
**Tier:** Foundations (Rookie)  
**Duration:** 6 minutes  
**Unlocks:** After first LP position opened  
**Prerequisite:** Module 3

---

## 🎯 Realize (30 seconds)

Your dashboard has 12 numbers, 3 charts, and a blinking status badge. Most of it is noise.  
**You only need to understand 5 things to know if you're winning.**

---

## 📖 Explain (4 minutes)

### The 5 Numbers That Matter

| # | Field | What It Means | Green Flag | Red Flag |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Total Position Value** | What your LP is worth right now | Growing slowly | Dropping fast vs. market |
| 2 | **Fees Earned (24h)** | Your cut of today's trades | Any positive number | Zero for 3+ days (dead pool) |
| 3 | **APR / APY** | Annual return rate at current pace | >10% APY | <3% APY (not worth gas) |
| 4 | **Pool Share %** | Your ownership of the total pool | Stable or growing | Shrinking (others depositing more) |
| 5 | **Impermanent Loss %** | How much you "lost" vs. holding | < your fee earnings | > your fee earnings |

**Everything else is decoration.** Learn these 5 first. Add the rest later.

### Reading the Dashboard Step-by-Step

**Step 1: Check health in 5 seconds**
> Is Total Position Value > my initial deposit?  
> If yes → you're earning.  
> If no → check how long you've been in. Under 7 days? Normal. Over 30 days? Investigate.

**Step 2: Check earnings momentum**
> Are my 24h fees consistent?  
> 3 days in a row with fees = healthy pool.  
> Sporadic fees = low volume pool (consider switching).

**Step 3: Compare to alternatives**
> Is my APY better than a savings account? (>4%)  
> Is it better than staking ETH? (~3-4%)  
> If yes to both → you're in the right place.

### The Status Badge Decoder

| Badge | Meaning | Your Action |
|---|---|---|
| 🟢 **Active** | Earning fees, position healthy | Do nothing |
| 🟡 **Idle** | No recent trades, low volume | Monitor for 48h |
| 🔵 **Compounding** | Auto-compound just fired | Check if gas cost < compound value |
| 🟠 **Rebalance Scheduled** | AAE will adjust your range soon | Review the new range parameters |
| 🔴 **Out of Range** | Price moved outside your LP range | Wait for rebalance or manually adjust |

### Common Dashboard Mistakes

❌ **Mistake 1:** Panicking because "Total Value" dropped 5% on a red day.  
✅ **Reality:** The whole market dropped 5%. Your *share* of the pool didn't change. You're fine.

❌ **Mistake 2:** Obsessing over hourly fee fluctuations.  
✅ **Reality:** Fees depend on trading volume. Some hours are dead. Look at 7-day averages.

❌ **Mistake 3:** Comparing your position to Twitter screenshots of $500/day earners.  
✅ **Reality:** They have $50K+ in capital. You're running your own race. See Module 5 for capital-appropriate expectations.

## ⚡ Act (1 minute)

Open your AAE dashboard right now and identify:
1. Your **Total Position Value**

4. Your current **Status Badge**

Screenshot or write them down. These are your baseline numbers. You'll compare against them weekly.

---

## ✅ Prove (30 seconds)

**Quiz:**

1. **Your Total Position Value dropped 3% today, but the overall crypto market dropped 4%. What does this mean?**
   - A) Your position is broken
   - B) You're actually outperforming the market by 1% ✅
   - C) You should immediately withdraw

2. **You haven't earned fees in 48 hours. What's the likely reason?**
   - A) The pool is stealing your money
   - B) Low trading volume in that pool ✅
   - C) Your deposit was too small

3. **Which metric tells you if impermanent loss is hurting you?**
   - A) Total Position Value
   - B) Pool Share %
   - C) Impermanent Loss % compared to Fees Earned ✅

**Passing:** 2/3 correct

## 💡 Pro Tip

Set a weekly calendar reminder: "Check LP dashboard." Not daily. Weekly. Daily checking leads to emotional decisions. Weekly checking gives you trend data. The best traders are patient traders.
