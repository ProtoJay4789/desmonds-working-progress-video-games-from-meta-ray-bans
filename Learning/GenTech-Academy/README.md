## Lesson Format

Every module follows the **REAP** structure:

- **R**ealize — Hook: Why this matters in 30 seconds
- **E**xplain — Core concept in plain English (no jargon without definition)
- **A**ct — Do this right now in the app
- **P**rove — 3-question quiz to lock it in

---

## Assessment Rules

- Quiz passing score: 2/3 questions (66%)
- Unlimited retakes — we're learning, not gatekeeping
- Wrong answers get instant explanation, not just "Incorrect"
- Tier advancement requires 80% of modules complete + 5-day streak
