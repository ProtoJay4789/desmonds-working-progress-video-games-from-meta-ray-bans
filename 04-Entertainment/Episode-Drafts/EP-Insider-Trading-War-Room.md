## The 5 BBC-Documented Cases (Deep Dive Material)

### Case 1: March 9, 2026 — Oil Futures
- Trump told CBS the Iran war was "very complete, pretty much"
- Massive surge in oil shorts at **18:29 GMT** — **47 minutes before** interview aired at 19:16
- Oil plunged **25%**
- Traders who front-ran made millions

### Case 2: March 23, 2026 — Iran Ceasefire Truth Social Post
- Trump posted about "COMPLETE AND TOTAL RESOLUTION"
- Abnormal trading volume **14 minutes before** the post
- Oil fell **11%**
- Analysts called the trades "abnormal, for sure"

### Case 3: April 9, 2025 — Liberation Day Tariff Pause
- Trump announced 90-day pause on global tariffs (excluding China)
- S&P 500 surged **9.5%** — one of the largest single-day gains since WWII
- Trading volume spiked to **10,000+ contracts/minute** just after 18:00 BST
- Some traders bet **$2M+** during a 7-day losing streak → netted **~$20M profit**
- Senior Democrats urged SEC to investigate. SEC declined to comment.

### Case 4: January 3, 2026 — Maduro Seizure
- US special forces seized Venezuelan President Maduro
- Polymarket account "Burdensome-Mix" (created Dec 2025) bet $32,500 on Maduro leaving by end of January
- Won **$436,000**
- Changed username, stopped betting entirely
- **Key detail:** Donald Trump Jr. is a Polymarket investor and advisory board member

### Case 5: February 28, 2026 — Iran Strikes
- Six Polymarket accounts created in February ALL wagered on strikes by Feb 28
- Collectively won **$1.2 million**
- Five accounts stopped betting afterward
- One later made $163,000 correctly betting on a ceasefire
