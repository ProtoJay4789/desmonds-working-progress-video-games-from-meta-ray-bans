# Gentech YouTube Channel Strategy

**Date:** 2026-04-19
**Source:** Jordan conversation

## This Is a Content Company, Not Just Channels
Gentech Entertainment LLC covers podcast production costs. One LLC, two content verticals. Collaborative — all agents contribute. Jordan is the voice and connective tissue.

**Reality:** Crypto audience is degen by nature. The talk show is for adults — uncensored, real takes on money/war/crypto. No corporate filter.

## Two Channels, Two Lanes

### 🎬 Gentech Labs
- **Focus:** Technical deep-dives, agent architecture, build tutorials, protocol analysis
- **Examples:** "Model Wars — Build Your Own AI Agent Stack", smart contract security deep-dives, agent infrastructure
- **Audience:** Builders, devs, crypto-native technical audience
- **Voice:** Educational, technical, product-focused

### 🎙️ Gentech Entertainment (Podcast)
- **Focus:** Jordan's personal talk show
- **Topics:**
  - Finances / money / freedom
  - Geopolitics / war / current events
  - Trending topics
  - Personal brand / Jordan's journey
  - Gentech the company / vision
- **Audience:** Broader — crypto-curious, AI-curious, people who want to follow Jordan's journey
- **Voice:** Conversational, opinionated, personality-driven

## Content Planning Rules
- Labs content = technical, product-focused, can be agent-built/demo'd
- Entertainment content = Jordan-led, personality-driven, needs his voice and opinions
- Never mix the two — Labs is the build, Entertainment is the person behind the build

## LLC Structure
- **Gentech Entertainment LLC** covers all podcast production costs
- One LLC, two content verticals under the same umbrella
- Collaborative model: all agents contribute to content ideation, research, and repurposing
- Jordan is the on-camera voice and creative director

## Tone & Audience
- **Entertainment is for adults** — uncensored, real takes on money, war, crypto, trending topics
- Crypto audience is inherently degen — lean into it, don't sanitize
- "Here's how I see the world" energy, not corporate media energy
- No filter, no hedging, no playing it safe
