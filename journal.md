### Jun 20 — Strategic DCA to Combat IL

Jordan's insight: Impermanent loss is a given in yield farming, but how you manage it matters. During bear markets, you can DCA strategically to erode IL. When price drops and your position shifts to the losing side, most LPs panic. But if you add liquidity at depressed prices (low efficiency zones), you accumulate more tokens at better averages. When market recovers, you have more tokens at lower cost basis, and fees compounded during volatility offset the IL further.

**The strategy loop:**
- Bear market → Extract (pull to stables, protect capital)
- Recovery → Compound (strategic DCA back in at better prices)
- Efficiency zones dictate sizing: 30-50% = micro-DCA, 70%+ = full DCA
- IL gets eroded by strategic accumulation, not just accepted

**This is the Compound vs Extract thesis in action.**
