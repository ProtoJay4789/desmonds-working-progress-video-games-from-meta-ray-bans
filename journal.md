### Jun 20 — GenTech Journal as Consumer Product

**Concept:** A journal where you think out loud. Write notes, discuss ideas, vent, brainstorm — and it visualizes your thoughts like a living map.

**The Vision:**
- Consumer-facing version of what we do with Obsidian vaults
- Data view shows your thoughts as a giant tangled web — looks like planet Earth from the outside
- Click a region → see your ideas. Click another → see your work stuff. Another → venting.
- Not a streak tracker. Not a to-do list. A place to *think out loud*
- Reparathy (AI companion) gives it a voice — she reflects, advises, makes it feel alive

**Tagline:** *"A journal where you can think out out loud."*

**Why it works:**
- Most people don't think to organize their thoughts this way
- The visual layer makes it feel alive, not like a boring note app
- It's personal — your brain, your map, your voice
- Social layer next (Reddit stories × visual dashboard)

**Status:** Concept. Needs wireframes and a name.
