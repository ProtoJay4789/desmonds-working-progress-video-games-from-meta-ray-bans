2. **x402 is the dominant pattern** — pay-per-request without accounts
3. **MCP (Model Context Protocol)** is the integration point — monetizing MCP servers
4. **Crowdedness score: 325** — dense cluster, but mostly demos/prototypes
5. **Gap: Production-ready** — most are hackathon demos, not live products
