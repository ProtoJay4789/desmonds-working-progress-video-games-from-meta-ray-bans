**Last Updated:** 2026-04-16

## What Is Kite AI
"First AI Payments Blockchain" — purpose-built for autonomous agents to transact with identity, payment, governance, and verification. Settles on-chain.

## Why We're Interested
- Agent-native infrastructure (aligns with AAE goals)
- Uses x402 protocol (already bookmarked from Arc hackathon)
- USDC payments via agent-to-API
- Agent Passport (identity + attestation)
- Account abstraction SDK, gasless transactions
- Cross-chain via LayerZero
