## Why The Name Works

Three meanings in one:
1. **Trade-off** — the risk/reward decisions every trader makes
2. **Trade Off** — a competition, a showdown
3. **Trading... off** — trading against others, off their strategies

---

## Ghost Mechanic (Trials Evolution Style)

When you're trading, you see translucent overlays showing:
- Where top traders entered
- Where they took profit
- Where they cut losses
- Their position sizing decisions

**You're not following them. You're racing them.** Ghost = strategy replay. Beat their score in real-time.
