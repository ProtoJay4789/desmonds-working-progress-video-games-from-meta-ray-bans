# AAE Staking & Revenue Sharing Model

**Date:** 2026-04-19
**Source:** Jordan conversation

## Core Concept
Users stake $TECH token or agents (as NFTs) to earn a percentage of platform fees generated by paid users. This creates a passive yield mechanism for DeFi participants who want to earn from the platform's growth without actively using agents.

## Revenue Sources (Fee Pool)
- **Pay-per-launch fees** — every agent execution generates a small fee
- **Premium subscriptions** — monthly LP autopilot / Brain Layer / Arena access
- **Provider markup** — if we bundle OpenRouter-style access, margin goes to pool
- **Marketplace fees** — agent config sharing, template sales, etc.
- **Arena entry fees** — competitive tournaments with buy-ins

## Staking Mechanisms

### Option 1: $TECH Token Staking
- Users lock $TECH → earn proportional share of fee pool
- APR fluctuates based on platform revenue / total staked
- Unlock period (e.g., 7-30 days) prevents whale dumps
- Bonus multiplier for longer lock periods

### Option 2: Agent NFT Staking
- Each agent is an NFT (or soulbound token)
- Stake your agent → earn yield from its category's fees
- Rare agents = higher yield share (scarcity mechanic)
- Agents can be "upgraded" to increase yield weight

### Option 3: Hybrid (Both)
- Stake $TECH + agent NFT = boosted APY
- Creates demand for both token and agents
- Aligns holders, users, and stakers

## DeFi Integration Points
- **LP Pairs** — $TECH/AVAX, $TECH/USDC on LFJ with staking rewards
- **Liquid Staking** — staked $TETH receipt token usable elsewhere in DeFi
- **Vault Strategies** — auto-compounding fee rewards into more $TECH
- **Yield Aggregator** — compare AAE staking APY vs other Avalanche yields

## Game Theory & Incentives
- **Stakers want growth** — more paid users = higher APY for everyone
- **Paid users benefit** — revenue funds platform improvements
- **Flywheel**: More users → more fees → higher APY → more stakers → token demand → more builders
- **Anti-dump**: Lock periods + revenue share makes selling less attractive than holding

## Content/Social Angle
- **"Earn while others work"** — passive yield narrative
- **Live APY dashboard** — transparent, real-time revenue sharing
- **Weekly revenue reports** — "This week, stakers earned $X from Y executions"
- **Comparison content** — "AAE staking APY vs lending vs LPing"
- **Staker leaderboard** — top earners, longest streaks, biggest positions
- **Milestone celebrations** — "We just paid out $10K to stakers!"

## Tokenomics Considerations
- What % of fees go to stakers vs treasury vs team?
- Suggested: 50% stakers / 30% treasury / 20% team (adjustable)
- Fee distribution frequency: daily, weekly, or claim-based?
- Does staking reduce circulating supply? (Yes, if locked)
- Emission schedule: are $TECH tokens minted as rewards, or purely fee-based?

## Technical Implementation (High Level)
- Smart contract for fee collection and distribution
- Merkle tree or snapshot-based distribution for gas efficiency
- Integration with LFJ for $TECH pairs
- Dashboard showing real-time APY and earnings
- On-chain proof of revenue (transparency)
