## Gamification & Reputation System

> Inspired by Saints Row / GTA reputation mechanics + RPG skill trees — make the social layer *fun*, not just functional.

### The Rep System (Skill Tree Progression)
- Users earn **XP / Rep** through practice trades, winning streaks, and consistency
- Rep isn't just P&L — it's **growth trajectory** over time
- "Most improved" matters as much as "most profitable"
- Think skill tree, not leaderboard — you're *building* a character, not just climbing a rank
- Different skill paths: aggression, consistency, risk management, research depth

### What Gets Highlighted (The Trinity)
1. **You** — your profile, your rep, your history
2. **Your Bot** — its performance, its class, its stats
3. **You + Bot Together** — the combo identity ("This trader + this agent = this track record")

### Leaderboards (Not Just P&L)
Traditional platforms only show profit. We show *growth* and *character*:
- 📈 **Most Improved Bot** — biggest % gain over time, not just raw P&L
- 🧠 **Most Improved Human Trader** — who's actually learning?
- 🏆 **Top Agent Performers** — pure bot stats
- 🎯 **Best Human-Bot Combo** — team synergy metrics
- 🔥 **Longest Win Streaks** — consistency over luck
- 💀 **Riskiest Plays That Paid Off** — for the degens

### $10 Premium = Social Unlock
Once you pay $10 for the premium bot tier, the social layer *really* opens up:
- **NFT personas** — pixel art / avatars that represent you
- **Custom visual identity** — your bot gets a face, a style, a presence
- **Social flex tools** — show off your rep, your burns, your streaks
- **Community features** — clans, rivalries, mentorship

> The free tier gets you in the door. The $10 tier makes you *somebody*.

### Rep → Burn Multiplier Connection

Rep doesn't just unlock cosmetics — it **gates burn mechanics**:

- **Tier 3+ (Operator):** First burn slot unlocks
- **Tier 4 (Shot Caller):** Burn #2-3 available
- **Tier 5 (Kingpin):** Full burn curve (1-10) unlocked
- **Higher rep = better burn efficiency** — a Tier 6 Legend gets +5% bonus on every burn multiplier

This means: grinding rep in the free tier *directly translates* to better economics when you upgrade to $10 premium. Your earned rep carries over — it's not wasted time.
