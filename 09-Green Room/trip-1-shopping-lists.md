## 📋 Strategy: Pack Light, Shop Local

**Key Insight (from Jordan):** Philippines has great shopping at night markets, street vendors, and local stores. Pack light on basics, buy当地 (locally) for things like clothes, toiletries, and beach gear.

### What to PACK (buy before leaving US)
- Electronics, documents, essentials that are cheaper/better in the US
- Items she can't easily find in Philippines

### What to BUY in Philippines
- Clothes (cheaper, lighter, tropical-appropriate)
- Beach gear (towels, rash guards, snorkel gear)
- Toiletries (travel sizes, local brands)
- Souvenirs and pasalubong (gifts)

## 🛍️ Shopping Strategy: What to Buy WHERE

### BUY IN US (before trip)
| Item | Why | Budget |
|------|-----|--------|
| Suitcase for Christel | Better quality/selection in US | $30-50 |
| Headphones for Christel | Electronics cheaper in US | $20-100 |
| Power banks | Cheaper, more reliable | $15-25 |
| Sunscreen SPF 50 | Expensive in PH, bring from US | $8-12 |
| Prescription meds | Bring 30-day supply | Varies |
| Rain jacket | Better selection in US | $20-40 |

### BUY IN PHILIPPINES (cheaper, lighter, local)
| Item | Where | Price Range |
|------|-------|-------------|
| T-shirts | Night markets, SM Mall | ₱150-300 ($3-5) |
| Shorts | Night markets, Divisoria | ₱200-400 ($4-7) |
| Beach gear | Beach shops, SM Mall | ₱300-500 ($5-9) |
| Toiletries | 7-Eleven, Watsons | ₱50-200 ($1-4) |
| Hair accessories | Night markets | ₱50-100 ($1-2) |
| Sarong/cover-up | Beach vendors | ₱200-400 ($4-7) |
| Local snacks | Everywhere | ₱20-100 ($0.40-2) |
| Pasalubong (gifts) | Markets, SM Mall | ₱100-500 ($2-9) |

### NIGHT MARKETS (Makati)
- **Salcedo Saturday Market** — artisanal food, crafts, clothing
- **Legazpi Sunday Market** — similar vibe, different vendors
- **Poblacion night market** — street food, clothes, accessories
- **Divisoria** — bargain shopping capital (take Grab there)
