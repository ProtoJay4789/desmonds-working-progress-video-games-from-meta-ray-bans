## Brand Concept

### The Hand Gesture
Two AI agents facing each other, one with the iconic "about to roast you" hand raised — that Black community roast gesture where you see the hand go up and you know what's coming. Combined with financial trading elements (charts, coins, suit/tie).

### Visual Identity
- **Colors:** Red/orange (roast/heat) + green (money/profit) + dark background
- **Mascot:** Agent character in a suit with the roast hand gesture
- **Style:** Cartoon/illustrated, not corporate — fun, viral, shareable
- **Font:** Bold, playful, not serious

### Why "Trade Roast" Works
1. **Memorable** — Two words, punchy, easy to say
2. **Descriptive** — Tells you exactly what it is
3. **Viral** — "Roast me" is already a cultural meme
4. **Distinctive** — No competitor has this angle
5. **Expandable** — Works for game, token ($ROAST), community, content

## The Reinforcement Loop

**Core Insight:** People learn by doing. People love doing what they love. Roasting is the entry point — the punishment for bad trades IS the game.

### The Loop
```
Trade badly → Get roasted → Feel the pain → Learn the lesson → Trade better → Get praised → Want more
     ↑                                                                                    │
     └────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘
```

### Why It Works
1. **Emotional anchor** — The roast makes the lesson stick ("I'll never forget Steve Harvey yelling at me for buying the top")
2. **Social proof** — Shareable roast cards = free marketing
3. **Retention** — "My bot told me X" is a story worth coming back for
4. **Competition** — Leaderboard of best/worst traders + best roasts
5. **Learning** — Pattern detection turns roasts into insights

### The Cultural Connection
- Black community roast culture → Universal appeal
- "Boy, what is WRONG with you?" → Everyone's heard this
- The hand gesture → Iconic, recognizable, memeable
- Roasting as love → Not mean-spirited, but constructive

## Game Mechanics (Refined)

### Tier System
| Tier | Name | Requirement | Perks |
|------|------|-------------|-------|
| Bronze | Rookie | Start | Basic roasts, limited leverage |
| Silver | Trader | 10 trades, 40% win rate | Unlock bot advisors |
| Gold | Pro | 50 trades, 55% win rate | Premium roasts, higher leverage |
| Diamond | Legend | 100 trades, 60% win rate | Custom roasts, governance voting |

### Roast Severity Scale
| Level | Trigger | Example |
|-------|---------|---------|
| Light | Minor mistake | "That's okay, we all have those days" |
| Medium | Bad trade | "Boy, what is WRONG with you?" |
| Hard | Terrible trade | "Back to the drawing board. For real." |
| Legendary | Catastrophic loss | Steve Harvey silence + head shake |

### Shareable Roast Cards
- Stats + AI roast text + agent avatar
- One-click share to X/Telegram
- "I got roasted by Steve Harvey for losing $500 in a bull market"
- Viral loop: friends see it → want to get roasted → connect wallet
