## What We're Testing

- Can an AI agent make complex blockchain concepts accessible to self-taught developers?
- What teaching methods work best? (visual, code-first, analogy, hands-on)
- Where do traditional bootcamps fail students like Jordan?
- What would an "anti-intimidation" coding education look like?

## Product Potential

- **Content library** — exercises, explanations, examples generated during the summer
- **Marketing story** — "I struggled with code until I built my own AI tutor"
