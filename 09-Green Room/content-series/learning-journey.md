## The Series

Jordan documents his journey learning smart contract development — from Cyfrin basics to Encode bootcamps to security audits. Authentic, relatable, educational.

## Core Philosophy: Augment, Don't Replace

**Jordan is an orchestrator who retains his skills.** AI doesn't think for him — it helps him think better.

> "I'm not outsourcing my brain. I'm using AI to strengthen my weaknesses, not replace my strengths."

**This is the marketing message:**
- ❌ "Let AI do it for you" — you learn nothing
- ✅ "Let AI help you do it yourself" — you learn, retain, grow
- Most AI tools sell the first. We sell the second.

**Content angle:**
- Document how Gentech helps Jordan improve his weaknesses
- Show the learning process, not just the results
- "I struggled with this → AI explained it → now I can do it myself"
- Authentic growth arc, not "AI did everything"
