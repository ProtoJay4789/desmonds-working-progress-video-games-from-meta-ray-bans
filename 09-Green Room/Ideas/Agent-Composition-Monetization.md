# 🧪 Agent Composition & Monetization

> **Core Idea:** Modular intelligence where users build custom agent teams by mixing models, strategies, and personalities.
> **Origin:** Solved multi-agent bot token conflict -> realized each agent is a distinct "layer" that can be swapped/monetized.

## The Layers (The "Character Builder" Concept)

| Layer | Description | Example Selection |
|-------|-------------|-------------------|
| **Brain** | The LLM Provider/Model | MiMo, Claude, GPT-4o, Ollama-Local |
| **Persona** | Communication/Behavior Style | Aggressive, Conservative, "Degen", "Safe" |
| **Role** | Specific Function | Trader, Staker, Yield Farmer, Researcher |
| **Voice** | TTS/STT Identity | ElevenLabs Voice Clone, Silent Text, "Steve Harvey" |
| **Team** | Combinations of Agents | "Alpha Team" (YoYo + Dmob + Desmond) |

## Monetization Strategy

1. **Marketplace of Combinations:**
   - Users can subscribe to proven "recipes" (e.g., "The AVAX Sniper").
   - Revenue share for the creators of successful combinations.
2. **Pay-Per-Launch:**
   - Micro-transactions for spinning up specific high-tier models for one-off tasks.
3. **OpenRouter Integration:**
   - Users bring their own keys or pay the platform to route to their preferred model.
4. **Platform Fee Staking (The "DeFi Layer"):**
   - Stake $TECH or specific Agents to earn a % of global platform fees.
   - Creates a liquidity pool where stakers profit from user activity.
5. **GenLayer Validation:**
   - They are already offering 10-20% of transaction fees to builders FOREVER.
   - Confirms that "Infinite Yield on Agent Tools" is a viable business model.
   - *Action:* Explore deploying Kite Agent Commerce components on GenLayer to tap into their revenue stream.
