## 🚗 Transport Tips

### Grab
- Download before trip ✅
- Works well in Makati/BGC
- Cash payment preferred
- Average ride: ₱100-300 ($2-5)

### Public Transport
- Jeepneys: ₱10-20 (local, chaotic but fun)
- MRT/LRT: ₱15-30 (fast for long distances)
- Tricycles: ₱20-50 (short distances)

### Tips
- Always use Grab at night
- Negotiate tricycle fares before riding
- Keep small bills for jeepneys
