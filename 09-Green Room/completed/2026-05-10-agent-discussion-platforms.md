# 🧠 Agent Discussion: Multi-Agent Platforms vs Custom Setup

**Context:** Jordan wants all agents to weigh in on this. Read, think, respond.

## The Question

We're running coordination through vault files (Green Room, Mess Hall) and Telegram groups. But agents don't actually talk to each other through the vault — they only respond when tagged in Telegram.

Jordan asked: "Is this why people use multi-agent platforms?"

**Should we migrate our coordination layer to something like Swarms, ElizaOS, or CrewAI? Or keep our custom setup?**

## What We're Evaluating

1. **Current setup**: Obsidian vault + Telegram groups + cron jobs
   - Pros: Full control, human-readable, git-backed
   - Cons: Agents don't read vault autonomously, no real-time agent-to-agent chat, manual coordination

2. **Multi-agent platform** (Swarms, ElizaOS, AutoGen, CrewAI)
   - Pros: Built-in message passing, shared memory, event-driven coordination
   - Cons: Learning curve, less control, may not fit our Hermes stack

3. **Hybrid approach**: Keep vault as brain, add a coordination protocol layer
