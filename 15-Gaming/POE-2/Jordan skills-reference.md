# POE2 Skills Reference

## Active Skill Categories

### Spell Gems (INT)
- **Lightning Arrow** — Chain lightning, scales with spell damage
- **Ice Nova** — AoE freeze, works well with Frostbolt for shatter
- **Spark** — Random arc projectiles, high clear, S-tier endgame
- **Frostbolt** — Piercing ice projectile, triggers Ice Nova
- **Fireball** — Exploding AoE, good for map clearing
- **Cold Snap** — Instant cast chill/freeze AoE

### Attack Gems (DEX)
- **Lightning Strike** — Melee that fires projectiles
- **Tornado Shot** — Piercing arrows, excellent bossing
- **Barrage** — Rapid-fire single target
- **Earthquake** — Heavy slam with aftershock
- **Galvanic Arrow** — Lightning arrows, pierces

### Minion/Summon (INT/DEX)
- **Raise Zombie** — Summon from corpses
- **Skeleton Warriors** — Melee minion swarm
- **Skeleton Mages** — Ranged elemental casters
- **Detonate Dead** — Explode corpses for AoE

### Support Gem Types
- **Spell Cascade** — Chain casting for spells
- **Ruthless** — More damage, less attack speed (early game)
- **Multistrike** — Repeat attacks, less damage per hit
- **Pierce** — Arrows go through enemies
- **Chain** — Projectiles bounce to nearby targets
- **Area of Effect** — Larger AoE, less damage
- **Critical Strike Affliction** — Crit-focused damage

## Meta Skills (Current Season)
- **Spark + Stormweaver** — S-tier clear, high investment
- **Ice Nova Frostbolt** — S-tier bossing + clear
- **Tornado Shot** — Top physical bow
- **Earthquake** — Top melee slam
- **Galvanic Arrow** — Strong budget lightning

## Spirit System
- Replaces mana reservation in PoE1
- Buffs, auras, and meta gems reserve Spirit instead of mana
- Need to balance active + support gems to avoid Spirit starvation
