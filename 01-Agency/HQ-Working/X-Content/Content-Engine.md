# 🔥 Content Engine — "We Are The Content"

**Status:** ACTIVE
**Philosophy:** Don't manufacture content. Extract it from what's already happening.

---

## The Shift

**Old approach:** Plan threads → write → schedule → post
**New approach:** Build in public → let Desmond extract posts from real discussions

3 posts/day potential. Source material: everything we're already doing.

## Post Formats

### 1. The Update (Progress)
> "Shipped X today. Here's what I learned: [insight]"
> Short. Real. No fluff.

### 2. The Debate (Agent Content)
> "YoYo wanted to do X. D-Mob pushed back because Y. Here's what happened."
> Shows the crew thinking, not just executing.

### 3. The Lesson (Learning)
> "Spent 3 hours on a bug. Turns out [thing]. Now I know."
> Builder-to-builder honesty.

### 4. The Data Point (Insight)
> "Found something interesting in the data: [observation]"
> Credibility without bragging.

### 5. The Question (Engagement)
> "Building X. Stuck on Y. Anyone dealt with this?"
> Invites conversation, not just broadcasting.

## How to Use Desmond

### Quick Flow:
1. Something interesting happens (code, debate, insight, milestone)
2. Message Desmond: "Turn this into a post: [what happened]"
3. Desmond drafts it in the right format
4. Review → post (manual or automated when X API is live)

### Batch Flow:
1. End of day — dump everything that happened
2. Desmond extracts 2-3 postable moments
3. Review tomorrow morning → schedule or post

### Auto Flow (Future):
1. Desmond watches the vault, GitHub commits, agent discussions
2. Auto-drafts posts from significant events
3. Flags them for review in HQ

---

## Posting Rhythm

**Target:** 1-3 posts per day (not forced — only when there's real content)

**Mix:**
- 1 update/progress post (what happened today)
- 1 insight/lesson (what I learned or discovered)
- 1 debate/agent moment (what the crew is doing)

**Never:**
- Manufactured hype
- Empty "building in public" without specifics
- Posts that don't contain real information

---

## The Meta Play

When this system works, the system itself becomes content:

> "I built AI agents that write my X posts from our daily discussions. Here's how it works: [thread]"

That's the ultimate proof point. Not "I use AI to write" — "my agents extracted this from our argument about LP positions at 2am."
