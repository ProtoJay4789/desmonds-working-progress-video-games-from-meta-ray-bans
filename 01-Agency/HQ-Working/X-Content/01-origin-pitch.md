## Core Pitch (refined)

**The problem:** People work 2-3 jobs. They don't have time to watch charts, catch dips, or manage positions. The market doesn't wait for your shift to end.

**The gap:** Existing solutions are either too complex (you need to be a quant), too expensive (managed funds eat your returns), or too risky (blindly following signals with no context).

**The solution:** A simple, safe, AI-powered trading agent that works while you work. You set the rules, it handles the execution.
