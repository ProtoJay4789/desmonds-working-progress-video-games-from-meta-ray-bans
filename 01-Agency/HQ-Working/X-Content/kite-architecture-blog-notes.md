## Core Architecture (Documented Live)

### The 8 Layers of a Kite Agent
1. 🧠 **Brain** — *What thinks* (model selection: MiMo, Claude, GPT-4o, local LLMs)
2. 🎭 **Personality** — *How it communicates* (tone, style, risk appetite expression)
3. ⚖️ **Enforcement** — *What it's allowed to do* (guardrails, compliance, risk limits)
4. 📝 **Memory** — *What it remembers and learns from* (knowledge base, learning style, time horizon)
5. 🎯 **Strategy** — *What playbook it runs* (yield farming, arbitrage, research analyst, degen sniper)
6. 🔗 **Tools** — *What it can actually touch* (chains, protocols, actions)
7. ⚡ **Execution** — *How it acts* (transaction construction, gas optimization, MEV protection, multi-sig routing)
8. 🧬 **Lifecycle** — *The agent's survival instinct* (burn floor awareness, revenue self-tracking, auto-downgrade signals, on-chain reputation, self-optimization)

### Key Principles (Jordan-confirmed)
- Every layer is independently swappable
- Clone any build → edit any layer → no locks anywhere
- User-configurable enforcement (tiered: free = defaults, premium = custom, pro = full policy engine)
- Leaderboard social meta-game: share configs, climb rankings, clone and tweak
- "Build while flying" — document additions as they emerge
