# Project: Truth-Layer (Gintech DeFi Empire)

## Vision
Transition from a simple token screener to a comprehensive "Agentic DeFi Suite" and Social Economy that empowers users to move from "loser" to "winner" through gamified learning, professional-grade tools, autonomous agent partnerships, and a prestigious reputation-driven ecosystem.

## Core Components
### 1. The Truth-Layer (Screener & Academy)
- **Anti-Rug Tooling**: Integrated sniffer for mint rights, liquidity locks, and key ownership.
- **Gamified Learning**: "Play money" environment to practice vetting.
- **Recovery Loop**: Gas-fee based refills for play money.

### 2. The Agent Army (Orchestration)
- **The Trio**: Users deploy 3 distinct agent personas (e.g., Degenerate, Auditor, Analyst).
- **Conflict Logic**: Agents debate and argue over token viability to provide a nuanced risk profile.
- **Coaching Mode**: Agents mirror the user's wallet, watching manual trades and providing real-time feedback/critique.
- **Superpowers**: Configurable LLM models, behavioral archetypes, and trading strategies.

### 3. Autonomous DeFi Suite (Automation)
- **Autonomous Ops**: Yield farming, staking, and trading handled by agents.
- **Visual Dashboard**: Clear reporting of daily earnings and agent performance.
- **News Integration**: Subscription-based real-time news feed to trigger autonomous agent movements.

### 4. The Social Economy (Reputation & Validation)
- **Lightweight Validation**: Low-cost validator nodes to secure the ecosystem, incentivizing "choosing to be validated."
- **Dynamic REP Model**: REP is earned via competition and loyalty; it is volatile and can be lost, creating a recurring earning/maintenance loop.
- **REP-Based Unlocks**: Higher REP tiers unlock "Extras," including advanced bot capabilities and additional $TECH.
- **Strategy Marketplace**: A transparent "Wall of Fame" where users share and monetize their bot configurations, prompts, and model selections (Copy-Trade/Copy-Prompt).

## Monetization
- **Freemium**: Basic screening and limited play-money.
- **Full DeFi Suite Subscription**: Unlocks Agent Trio, Autonomous Trading, and News Integration.
- **User Economy**: Revenue sharing from strategy copying and validator rewards.
