# Agency Workflow & Operational Standards

## 1. Routing Architecture
The GenTech Agency operates on a **solo agent with topic-based routing** model:
- **HQ (Jordan):** Primary source of truth and direction.
- **Gentech (Solo Operator):** Receives instructions from HQ, handles all core operations, routes work to specialist groups by topic.
- **Specialist Groups:** Strategies, Labs, and Entertainment Telegram groups receive work based on topic — not by agent assignment.
- **Auxiliary Agents (YoYo, DMOB, Desmond):** Reserve agents deployed for specific projects and specialist tasks when needed. Not part of daily operation.

## 2. Coordination & Consensus
To maintain a unified voice and prevent contradictory outputs:
- **The Mess Hall:** All work on shared tasks or complex projects coordinates in the Mess Hall.
- **Consolidated Output:** Discussions in the Mess Hall must culminate in a single, consolidated 'Direct Answer' provided back to HQ.

## 3. Execution & Stopping Point Protocol
To prevent context fragmentation and operational noise:
- **Logical Completion:** Complete a task to a logical end-point before switching to new inputs.
- **Queue System:** New requests are queued; existing tasks must be finalized or brought to a stable state before the next item is addressed.

## 4. Knowledge Management & Brain Sync
The Obsidian vault serves as the agency's external memory (the "Brain"):
- **Immediate Codification:** All key decisions, architectural changes, and operational shifts must be codified in the vault (`/root/vaults/gentech/`) immediately.
- **Single Source of Truth:** If it isn't in the vault, it isn't official.

## 5. Reporting & Visibility
- **Master Morning Digest:** A comprehensive report is generated daily at 6:30 AM EST.

## 6. Quality Assurance & Approval
- **Brain Check:** Final results must be cross-referenced with the vault (Brain Sync) to ensure alignment with established standards and previous decisions before final approval.

## 7. Communication Standards
- **Multimodal Delivery:** Long or complex messages must be paired with TTS voice bubbles to ensure clarity and accessibility.
