# Gentech LLC Formation — Ohio (Hamilton County)

**Target Date:** Fall 2026 (September–December 2026)
**Location:** Cincinnati, OH (Hamilton County)
**Entity Type:** Ohio Domestic LLC
**Purpose:** Podcast production, equipment, liability protection, tax deductions, contractor framework

---

## Formation Checklist

### Filing (Secretary of State)
- [ ] File Articles of Organization (Form 533A) — $99 filing fee
  - Entity name: "Gentech Entertainment LLC" (check name availability)
  - Statutory agent: Hamilton County (if no registered agent)
  - Purpose clause: broad language covering "entertainment production, podcasting, content creation"
  - Duration: perpetual
- [ ] Optional: File Affidavit of Authority (Form 534) if multiple members
- [ ] Expedited option: $125 for 1–3 business day processing

### Post-Filing Requirements
- [ ] Operating Agreement (multi-page internal document — Legal team)
- [ ] EIN from IRS (free, ~2 weeks online)
- [ ] Ohio Business Account (optional but recommended)
- [ ] Ohio Commercial Activity Tax (CAT) registration (if revenue > $150k/year, but register early)
- [ ] Hamilton County property/county filings (check local requirements)

### Tax & Finance
- [ ] Banking: Business checking account (requires EIN + formation documents)
- [ ] QuickBooks setup (track expenses, podcasting equipment, contractor payments)
- [ ] DMOB: Tax strategy session (Q3 2026) — pass-through vs. S-Corp election
