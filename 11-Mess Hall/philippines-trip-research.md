## Best Time to Visit

| Window | Weather | Crowds | Vibe |
|--------|---------|--------|------|
| **November-December** ⭐ | Perfect. Dry season starts, warm | Fewer than peak holidays | Holiday vibes, great weather |
| **April** | Driest, sunniest | Moderate | Holy Week festivals, island-hopping ideal |
| **Peak (Dec-Feb)** | Best weather | Highest prices, most crowded | Christmas celebrations |

**Recommendation:** November-December for perfect weather + value. April for dry season + festivals.

## Top 10 Islands/Destinations (Crew Trip)

1. **Boracay** — White sand beaches, nightlife, water sports
2. **El Nido, Palawan** — Lagoons, limestone cliffs, island-hopping
3. **Coron, Palawan** — Shipwreck diving, hot springs, Twin Lagoon
4. **Cebu** — Moalboal sardine run, Oslob whale sharks, waterfalls
5. **Siargao** — Surfing capital (Cloud 9), lagoons, island-hopping
6. **Bohol** — Chocolate Hills, tarsiers, Alona Beach
7. **Siquijor** — Mystical island, beaches, waterfalls, less touristy
8. **Batanes** — Northernmost, dramatic landscapes, cultural immersion
9. **Puerto Galera** — Diving, nightlife, closer to Manila
10. **Camiguin** — Volcanic island, hot springs, fewer crowds

## Cost of Living: Cincinnati vs Metro Manila

**You'd need ~$3,000/month in Manila** to maintain the same lifestyle as **$5,400/month in Cincinnati** (~44% cheaper)

| Category | Manila | Cincinnati |
|----------|--------|------------|
| Local restaurant meal | $2-3 | $15-18 |
| Beer at a bar | $1-2 | $6-8 |
| Airbnb/hotel per night | $30-60 | $100-150 |
| Grab ride | $2-5 | $10-20 Uber |
| Beach resort room | $50-100 | $200+ |
| Massage (1 hour) | $8-15 | $60-80 |
