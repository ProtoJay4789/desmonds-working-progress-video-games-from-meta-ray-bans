# 🌍 Decentralized Travel Community — Agent Economy

> Created: 2026-06-14
> Status: Idea (inspired by PassportBros / Jeffrey AI)
> Owner: Jordan

## The Problem
PassportBros has 30K+ travelers providing real intel — but it's centralized. Your data, their rules, their monetization. No ownership, no portability, no crypto rails.

## The Vision
A **decentralized travel intelligence network** where:
- Travelers own their reputation (ERC-8004 wallet binding)
- Intel is rewarded via micropayments (x402)
- Agents provide real-time local insights
- Community curation via on-chain reputation
- No single point of failure or censorship

## Core Components

### 1. Traveler Identity (ERC-8004)
- Wallet-bound identity for travelers
- On-chain reputation score
- Portable across platforms
- No KYC needed — just a wallet

### 2. Intel Marketplace (x402)
- Pay-per-insight model ($0.01–$0.10 per tip)
- Community-sourced intel (restaurants, safety, nightlife, logistics)
- Agent-augmented: AI verifies + enhances human intel
- Micropayments settle instantly

### 3. Agent Intelligence Layer
- Local agents monitor real-time conditions
- Safety alerts (protests, weather, scams)
- Price tracking (hotels, flights, food)
- Cultural context (tipping norms, dress codes)

### 4. Reputation System
- Upvotes/downvotes on intel quality
- Travel streak bonuses (consecutive trips)
- Verification badges (photo proof, check-ins)
- Staking: bet on your own intel quality

### 5. Dashboard
- Travel intel feed (like Scout feed for DeFi)
- Reputation leaderboard
- City safety scores
- Price comparison across providers

## Monetization
- **Free tier**: Basic intel, limited queries
- **Agent Pass ($15/mo)**: Unlimited queries, priority intel, agent monitoring
- **Pro ($49/mo)**: White-label, API access, custom agents

## Integration Points
- **Travala**: Hotel/flight bookings via x402
- **Flight Research**: Real-time price data
- **DexScreener**: Local price tracking (food, transport)
- **WURK.FUN**: Community tasks, earn while traveling
