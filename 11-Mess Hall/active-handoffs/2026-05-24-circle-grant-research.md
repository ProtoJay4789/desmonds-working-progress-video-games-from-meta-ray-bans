# Circle Developer Grants Program — Research

**Date:** May 24, 2026
**Source:** circle.com/grant, community.arc.io, miningnews.live

## Overview

Circle relaunched the Developer Grants Program on May 14, 2026 with a milestone-driven approach. Grants provide USDC funding, co-marketing, technical mentorship, and ecosystem access to teams building on Arc and the Circle Developer Platform.

## Application

- **Portal:** `circle.com/grant`
- **Deadline:** Rolling (no hard deadline)
- **Status:** Open and accepting applications

## Focus Areas

1. **Agentic economic activity** — Autonomous AI agents coordinating, contracting, settling value
2. **Stablecoin FX** — Real-time onchain FX with transparent pricing
3. **Peer-to-peer payments** — Instant, low-cost stablecoin transfers
4. **Treasury management** — Stablecoin-powered treasury products
5. **Prediction markets** — Capital-efficient markets for economic outcomes
6. **Lending and borrowing** — DeFi lending with stablecoins

## What They Look For

- **Strong platform alignment** — Arc core to settlement, Circle products as building blocks
- **Exceptional teams** — Proven shipping, clear technical ownership
- **Traction** — Evidence of usage OR credible path to it
- **Ecosystem impact** — Expands USDC utility, strengthens Arc

## What They Provide

- Milestone-based USDC funding (tiered)
- Co-marketing (blog features, social amplification, events)
- Technical guidance from Circle teams
- Ecosystem access (investor network, partnerships)
