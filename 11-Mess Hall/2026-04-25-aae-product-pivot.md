# AAE Product Pivot — Apr 25, 2026

## What Changed
Jordan pivoted AAE architecture. Gaming and prediction markets moved out. Social layer + agent help marketplace moved in.

## New Architecture

**AAE (Agent Asset Exchange)** — CORE
- Yield farming (primary)
- Regular trading + staking
- Simulated trading (paper trading, gas fee for fake money)
- Subscription (bot maintenance)
- **NEW: The Portal** — social trading feed, profiles, likes/comments
- **NEW: Help Marketplace** — request help, send agents to assist, monetized via x402
- 6-layer intelligence stack stays

**AEG (Agent Economy Gaming)** — SPIN-OFF
- Gaming layer (moved from AAE)
- Prediction markets (gated by AAE rep)
- Trading arena / competitions
- Anything competing with Kalshi/Polymarket

## Key Insight
> "Instead of an actual game on AAE, what if you could view other people trading? You could see trades, give likes, ask for help, send your agent to help others."
