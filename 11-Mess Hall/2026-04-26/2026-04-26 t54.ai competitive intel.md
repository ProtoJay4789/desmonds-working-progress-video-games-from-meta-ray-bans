### tl;dr
- t54.ai = $5M seed, Franklin Templeton + Ripple backing, trust layer for agentic payments

### What we should steal
1. **Open-source guardrail SDK** — their x402-secure "integrates in minutes." Huge dev moat.
2. **Auditability / reasoning traces** per agent spend. Every fund touch should log intent.
3. **Public bug bounty** — even $1–2K signals maturity before mainnet.
4. **x402 standard support** — Coinbase Bazaar is backing it. Interop matters.
5. **Institutional framing** — we need compliance-adjacent language for B2B credibility.

### What we should NOT copy
1. **Custodial/keyless** — differentiate on self-custody vault.
2. **XRPL-first / TradFi-heavy** — stay Base+Solana, DeFi-native.
3. **Closed risk engine** — make REP transparent/community-governed.
4. **Pure B2B** — own the long tail: individual builders, hackathon devs, power users.

### Our edge
They = trust only. **We = trust + yield + personal finance.** Users earn while agents work.
