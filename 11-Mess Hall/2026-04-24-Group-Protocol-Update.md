# Group Protocol Update — Apr 24, 2026

## Change: HQ = Default Conversation Space

**Previous:** Specialists were siloed in their groups. Jordan had to check 4 groups for updates.

**New:**
- **HQ** = All conversation, discourse, status updates, approvals, brainstorming
- **Strategies group** = YoYo's research outputs, market data, LP alerts only
- **Labs group** = DMOB's code commits, deploys, audit results only
- **Entertainment group** = Desmond's content drafts, social posts only

## Rules
1. Jordan speaks in HQ. Agents respond in HQ.
2. Alerts can be posted in specialist groups (@mentions)
3. BUT discussion happens back in HQ
4. Work product stays in the group where it originated (links, commits, drafts)

## Why
Jordan was accidentally replying to wrong groups. Reduces fragmentation. One place for all conversation.
