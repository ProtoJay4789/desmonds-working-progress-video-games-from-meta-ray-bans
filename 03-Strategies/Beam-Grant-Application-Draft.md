## What We're Building

AgentEscrow is a trustless escrow system designed specifically for AI agent transactions:

- **AI-validated escrow** — agents verify work quality before releasing funds
- **x402 nanopayments** — pay-per-use agent services (fractions of a cent)
- **Smart timing** — agents know when markets are active (US hours) vs. quiet (Asia overnight)
- **Pool health exits** — auto-withdraw when conditions deteriorate
- **One-address onboarding** — paste a token address, get full intelligence
