## Analysis: Why No Real Estate on AVAX?

1. **Operational complexity** — Property management, legal entities, jurisdiction compliance require off-chain infrastructure that small crypto teams can't build
2. **AVAX institutional focus** — Avalanche Labs has targeted institutional RWA (treasuries, credit) via partnerships with KKR, Franklin Templeton, Apollo — not retail real estate
3. **Centrifuge dominance** — $258M on AVAX is all credit/fund tokenization, not property
4. **Landshare proves the thesis failure** — Even on BSC with $621K TVL, the model couldn't sustain

## What's Actually Viable on AVAX for RWA Exposure

### Best Opportunities (Ranked)

1. **Centrifuge (CFG)** — $258M on AVAX, tokenized credit/funds. If you want RWA yield on AVAX, this is the deepest pool
2. **ONDO** (via multi-chain) — Tokenized treasuries, $28M daily volume. Available natively on multiple chains
3. **Republic Note (NOTE)** — $12.7M, AVAX-only. Venture/crypto portfolio exposure — closest to "diversified RWA" on AVAX
4. **Asseto AoABT** — $18M, AVAX-native. Fund share tokens — more institutional than retail
5. **Brickken (BKN)** — $41M, tokenized business assets. Could include real estate but primarily business equity

### Recommendation for Jordan

**The "tokenized real estate income" thesis is dead on AVAX.** There is no pipeline, no upcoming launch, and the operational barriers make it unlikely to appear soon.

**If you want RWA on AVAX:**
- Centrifuge (CFG) for credit/fund exposure
- Republic Note for diversified venture exposure
- Skip the real estate angle entirely until a credible team builds on AVAX

**If you want real estate tokenization specifically:**
- Lofty (Algorand) — largest, most active
- Tangible (Polygon) — real estate NFTs
- None have AVAX presence or bridging
