# GenTech Tokenomics & Product Stack: The "Prime" Model

## Mission
**"We want you to succeed in life. Use AI the right way."**
From bug bounties to building an ecosystem where users win in Gaming, Trading, Language Learning, and Travel.

---

## 1. The Product Stack (The "Prime" Bundle)
*Goal: Compound value so churn is impossible. One subscription covers everything.*

### A. TrustGuard (DeFi Safety)
*   **The Edge:** "Good Cop/Bad Cop" security agents.
*   **Features:** Portfolio monitoring, risk alerts, anti-tilt mechanisms.
*   **Target:** Crypto traders, DeFi users.

### B. Gaming Companions (Entertainment)
*   **The Edge:** AI agents that know your build, your history, and your playstyle.
*   **Features:** Real-time strategy, voice interaction, "Insider Trading" Roguelike.
*   **Target:** Gamers (POE2, MMOs), Competitive players.

### C. Autonomous Language Learning (ALL)
*   **The Edge:** Learn a language *through* your hobbies (e.g., "Spanish for Gamers").
*   **Features:** Contextual vocabulary, voice conversation, cultural nuance.
*   **Target:** Students, Travelers, Professionals.

### D. Travel Concierge (Agency)
*   **The Edge:** Personalized travel planning via AI agent.
*   **Features:** Itinerary building, flight/hotel coordination (via Dadrian).
*   **Target:** Everyone.

## 2. Subscription Tiers
*   **Basic ($5/mo):** Single Service access (e.g., TrustGuard only).
*   **Alpha ($10/mo):** Two services + Advanced features (e.g., TrustGuard + Gaming).
*   **Ultimate ($15/mo):** **The Full Stack.** All services, all features.
    *   *Value Proposition:* "Pay for one, get them all as we launch."

## 3. Token Economics ($TECH)

### Utility
1.  **Governance:** Vote on new features/verticals to add to the stack.
2.  **Discounts:** Pay annual subscriptions with $TECH for a 10-15% discount.
3.  **Access:** High-level alpha or beta features may require staking a minimum amount.

### Revenue Distribution (The Flywheel)
*Subscription revenue (USDC/Stripe) flows into the Treasury smart contract.*

*   **40% Operations:** Server costs, API keys, Agent maintenance.
*   **30% Staker Yield:** Distributed to $TECH stakers as **USDC** (Real Yield).
    *   *Why:* Users buy $TECH to get the yield. Creates buy pressure.
*   **30% Treasury Buybacks:** Treasury buys $TECH off the open market.
    *   **Option A:** Burn (deflationary).
    *   **Option B:** Distribute to stakers (increases yield).

### Why This Wins
*   **Real Value:** The token is backed by cash flow from real products.
*   **Compound Interest:** More products = Higher subscription value = More revenue = Higher yield.
*   **Sticky:** Users stay for the bundle; investors stay for the yield.
