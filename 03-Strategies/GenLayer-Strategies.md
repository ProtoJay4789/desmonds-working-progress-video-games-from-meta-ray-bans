# GenLayer Passive Income & Operations Strategy

## 🎯 Passive Income Paths
Based on research (`06-Content/genlayer-passive-income-research.md`):

### 1. Delegation (Low Risk / Low Friction) 🟢
- **Minimum**: 42 GEN
- **Requirements**: No infrastructure needed.
- **Expected Yield**: ~13.5% effective APR (starting ~15% inflation minus 10% validator cut), decaying toward 4% over time.
- **Mechanism**: Set-and-forget, auto-compounding.
- **Risk/Lockup**: 7-epoch unbonding (~7 days) + slashing risk.

### 2. Validator (High Yield / High Effort) 🟡
- **Minimum**: 42,000 GEN
- **Requirements**: Active node operation.
- **Yield**: Higher base rewards than delegation.
