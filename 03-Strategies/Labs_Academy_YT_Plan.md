# 📺 Labs Academy: Module 2 - DeFi Architecture
## Series Title: The Mathematics of Yield
**Target Audience:** Technical degens, aspiring quant devs, DeFi researchers.
**Style:** "Numbers-first." No hype, just logic and live data.

### Episode 1: Concentration & Liquidity (The LFJ Model)
- **Core Concept:** UniV3 / LFJ Concentrated Liquidity.
- **Technical focus:** Price ranges, tick spacing, and why "full range" is a waste of capital.
- **Visual Spec:** B&W plot of the liquidity curve. Highlight the "Active Range" vs "Out of Range" with high-contrast textures.
- **Live Case:** Our AVAX/USDC position.

### Episode 2: The Yield Curve & Impermanent Loss (IL)
- **Core Concept:** The trade-off between fee accrual and price divergence.
- **Technical focus:** Calculating IL in a concentrated range. The "Divergence Loss" vs "Fee Gain" equation.
- **Visual Spec:** A "Battle" chart: IL (Black) vs Trading Fees (White). Show the break-even point.
- **Live Case:** Backtesting our $TECH/USDC target ranges.

### Episode 3: Risk Management & Hedging
- **Core Concept:** Delta Neutrality and Portfolio Balancing.
- **Technical focus:** Using perps to hedge LP positions. The math of the "Delta."
- **Visual Spec:** A balancing scale visual—Asset on one side, Hedge on the other. High contrast, stencil style.
- **Live Case:** Our overall portfolio exposure across SOL, LINK, and AVAX.

### Episode 4: The Economy of $TECH (Tokenomics in Action)
- **Core Concept:** Token Sinks and Value Accrual.
- **Technical focus:** The burn/recycle mechanism. Comparing "Inflationary" vs "Deflationary" design.
- **Visual Spec:** Flow chart of $TECH flowing through the system. Use bold arrows and gritty textures to show "The Sink."
- **Live Case:** The dual-pricing model (USDC vs $TECH discount).

### Episode 5: On-Chain Monitoring (The Strategy Stack)
- **Core Concept:** Automated Oversight.
- **Technical focus:** How YoYo monitors LPs. Setting alerts for "Out of Range" and "Impermanent Loss" thresholds.
- **Visual Spec:** Terminal-style screen recording. Raw logs, JSON outputs, and a "War Room" dashboard.
- **Live Case:** Demo of the current hourly cron-job output.

### Episode 6: Capstone — Building a Sustainably Profitable Vault
- **Core Concept:** Synthesis.
- **Technical focus:** Putting it all together: Security (DMOB) + Architecture (YoYo). 
- **Visual Spec:** A blueprint-style map of the entire Gentech Labs strategy.
- **Live Case:** The roadmap for the next $1M of liquidity.
