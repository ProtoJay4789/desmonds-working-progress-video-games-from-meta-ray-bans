## 🔬 AAE Feature: Tokenomics Transparency Score

**Status:** Concept — "DYOR, but actually"

**What:** When a user inserts a ticker, show the tokenomics breakdown — not recommendations, just the facts so users can make informed decisions.

**What it shows:**
- 📊 Allocation breakdown — team, investors, treasury, community, ecosystem
- 🐋 Whale concentration — top 10 holders and % of supply
- 🔒 Vesting schedule — when insider tokens unlock, cliff dates, emission schedule
- ⚠️ Red flags — "80% held by 3 wallets" or "team vesting ends next month"
- 💰 Fully diluted valuation vs. current market cap (is it priced for 10x dilution?)

**What it doesn't do:**
- No buy/sell recommendations — just data
- No "this coin is good/bad" — let the user decide
- Shows the receipts so users can call bullshit themselves

**Why this matters:**
- Most retail never checks tokenomics before buying
- "I didn't know the team held 60%" is the #1 post-rug complaint
- WLFI / Trump token is the perfect example — insiders loaded, retail provides exit liquidity

**Content Angles:**
- [ ] "You bought a token without checking who holds it? Here's what happened."
- [ ] Side-by-side: healthy tokenomics vs. obvious insider dump setup
- [ ] "DYOR isn't reading the whitepaper. It's checking the allocation."
- [ ] Demo: insert $COIN → allocation chart → whale map → vesting timeline → red flags highlighted
