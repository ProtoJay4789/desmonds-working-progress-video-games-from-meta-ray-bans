- **PayAI Network (PAYAI)** — Solana x402 settlement layer we reference in `Strategies/birdeye-x402-api.md`
- **Zauthx402 (ZAUTH)** — Auth layer for x402 payments, overlaps with our AgentEscrow auth model
- **x402jobs, x4Pay, Bridge402, Sol402 Proxy** — Pure x402 infra plays at micro-cap; potential partnership or competitive intel

### Positioning Angles
- **AgentEscrow v2** sits at the trust/reputation layer above x402 payment rails
- No project on this list offers escrow + reputation + dispute resolution — our gap
- ERC-8004 spending permissions (see glossary) could differentiate us from simple x402 facilitators
