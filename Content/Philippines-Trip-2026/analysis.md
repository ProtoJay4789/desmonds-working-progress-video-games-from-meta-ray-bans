## 🌤️ Weather & Conditions

### June — Start of Wet Season
- **Rain:** Moderate (~15–20 rainy days/month, mostly afternoon)
- **Temp:** 27–32°C (80–90°F), humid
- **Typhoon risk:** Low-Moderate
- **Crowds:** Moderate (school's out domestically)

### July — Peak Wet Season
- **Rain:** Heavy (~18–22 rainy days/month, overcast)
- **Temp:** 26–31°C (79–88°F)
- **Typhoon risk:** Moderate
- **Crowds:** Moderate-High (Western summer holiday)
- **Prices:** Highest of the 4 months

### August — Peak Wet Continues
- **Rain:** Heavy (~18–22 rainy days/month, afternoon/evening downpours)
- **Temp:** 26–31°C (79–88°F)
- **Typhoon risk:** Moderate-High
- **Crowds:** Moderate
- **Prices:** Good value, dropping

### September ⭐ RECOMMENDED
- **Rain:** Moderate-Heavy (~15–18 rainy days/month, tapering)
- **Temp:** 26–31°C (79–88°F)
- **Typhoon risk:** Moderate (but storms less intense than Oct–Nov peak)
- **Crowds:** Low (off-peak, fewer tourists)
- **Prices:** Lowest of all months

## 🎯 September 2026 Recommendation

**Go September.** Here's why:

1. **Cheapest flights** — $799 from SFO, $869 from LAX
2. **Weakest peso in history** — USD goes 11% further than 2025
3. **Fewer crowds** — more authentic, better hotel deals
4. **Weather improving** — rains tapering off by late Sept
5. **30–45% cheaper** than June/July overall

### Risk Mitigation: Typhoon Season
- Most severe typhoons hit October–November, not September
- Monitor weather closer to date (PAGASA forecasts)
- Travel insurance with weather cancellation: ~$50–80
- Book flexible fares if budget allows
