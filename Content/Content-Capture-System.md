## Pipeline

1. **Listen**: Monitor all Gentech conversations (HQ, Labs, Strategies)
2. **Capture**: Extract the story, insight, or pivot
3. **Draft**: Write script/post in appropriate format
4. **Queue**: Save to `Content/Queue/` with metadata
5. **Produce**: When Jordan says "make this" or I see an opportunity
6. **Publish**: Deliver final asset + caption + hashtags
