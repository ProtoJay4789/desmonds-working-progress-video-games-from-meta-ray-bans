## The Quote
> "agentic commerce will be bigger than human commerce. let that sink in."

## Why It Matters
This is the core thesis behind the Agent Economy movement:
- AI agents buying, selling, negotiating, and transacting autonomously
- More transactions per agent than any human could execute
- 24/7 operation, no fatigue, instant execution
- Scales exponentially as agent populations grow

## GenTech Angle
This validates our entire roadmap:
- **AgentEscrow** — trust infrastructure for agent-to-agent transactions
- **TECH Payment Router** — economic layer for agentic commerce
- **REP system** — reputation for agents to transact safely
