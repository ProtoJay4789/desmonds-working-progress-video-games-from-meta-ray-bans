## 📖 Explain (2.5 minutes)

### What Is Gas?

Gas = the fee you pay to process any transaction on the blockchain.

Think of it like postage:
- Sending a letter (simple transfer) = cheap stamp
- Sending a package with insurance (complex smart contract) = expensive stamp
- During Christmas rush (network congestion) = even more expensive

**Every action costs gas:**
- Deposit into a pool: ~$1-5
- Claim fees: ~$1-3
- Rebalance position: ~$2-8
- Withdraw: ~$1-5

### The Small Capital Trap

| Capital | Daily Fees | Gas to Claim | Net After Gas | Reality |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| $100 | $0.50 | $2.50 | -$2.00 | ❌ You're losing money |
| $500 | $2.50 | $2.50 | $0.00 | ⚠️ Breaking even |
| $1,000 | $5.00 | $2.50 | +$2.50 | ✅ Profitable |
| $5,000 | $25.00 | $2.50 | +$22.50 | ✅ Very profitable |

**Rule of thumb:** Don't claim fees more than once per week if your position is under $1,000. Let them accumulate.

### Gas Optimization Strategies

**1. Time Your Transactions**
- Gas is cheapest: Weekends, late night US time (2-6 AM EST)
- Gas is most expensive: Weekday mornings, major news events
- Use a gas tracker to find the sweet spot

**2. Batch Your Actions**
- Don't deposit, then claim, then rebalance as 3 separate transactions
- AAE's rebalancer batches actions when possible — let it do the work

**3. Pick the Right Chain**
| Chain | Avg Gas per Transaction | Best For |
|---|---|---|
| Ethereum | $5-50 | Whales ($10K+) |
| Avalanche | $0.50-2 | Mid-cap ($1K-10K) |
| Arbitrum | $0.50-3 | Mid-cap |
| Base | $0.10-1 | Small-cap ($100-1K) |
| Solana | $0.001-0.01 | All sizes |

**4. Let Fees Compound**
- AAE auto-compounds when gas cost < 10% of compound value
- If you're small-cap, this might mean monthly compounding instead of daily
- That's fine. Time in the market beats timing the market.

### When Gas Is Your Friend

Gas fees actually protect you from yourself:
- High gas = harder to panic-sell on a red day
- Transaction costs force you to think before acting
- Small, frequent trades get eaten by fees → you naturally become more patient
