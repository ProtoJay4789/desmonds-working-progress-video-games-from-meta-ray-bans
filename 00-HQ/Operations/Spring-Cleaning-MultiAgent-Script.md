# Spring Cleaning — Multi-Agent Voice Script
**Date:** May 02, 2026  
**Format:** Instagram Story (60-90 seconds)  
**Concept:** Each agent "checks in" with their voice/perspective on today's bloat cleanup

---

## [SCENE 1 — GENTECH (CEO)]
*[Calm, measured, visionary tone]*

"Team, we've got a storage situation. 152 GB consumed — that's not just numbers, that's friction. Every megabyte we waste is velocity we're stealing from ourselves. 

Today we did the deep dive: 56 GB of recursive backup bloat, 75 GB of VideoAgent datasets loafing around. We diagnosed the problem, mapped the cleanup, and made the strategic call: hackathon first. Solana Frontier, Kite AI — those deadlines don't move.

So we document, we plan, and we execute on our timeline. This is what disciplined growth looks like."

## [SCENE 2 — YOYO (GROWTH)
*[Upbeat, rapid-fire, data-optimistic]*

"Okay but let's talk about what this actually MEANS, okay? 56 GB in nested backups? That's a VIRAL coefficient problem — our replication strategy is broken! And 75 GB of unused VideoAgent tools? That's a 40% opportunity cost on our media pipeline!

Once we clean this up, we're talking: faster CI/CD, better agent responsiveness, lower cloud costs — that's MARGIN we can reinvest into growth channels! I'm already mapping the efficiency gains!"

## [SCENE 3 — DMOB (TECH LEAD)
*[Pragmatic, systems-focused, slightly dry]*

"Y'all keep talking about it — let me give you the actual fix list. One: prune the recursive Hermes backup loops (that's a bug in the archiver). Two: archive VideoAgent datasets to cold storage, keep active datasets only. Three: clean build artifacts across all repos — Rust/Python/Node/Python caches are eating 3 GB alone.

After the hackathons, we run the cleanup, we fix the backup script, we set up monitoring. That's it. No drama."

## [SCENE 4 — DESMOND (CREATIVE)
*[Smooth, cultural, visionary]*

"See, this is the part where we level up. Storage bloat is creative debt — it's ideas we never got to, projects we started and abandoned, potential energy sitting on a disk.

Once we clear that mental space? Watch. The creative bandwidth we free up is worth more than the gigabytes. The cleanup isn't just technical — it's psychological. We're making room for what's next."
