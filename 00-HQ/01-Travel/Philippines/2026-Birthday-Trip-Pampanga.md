- **Clark (CRK) IS IN Angeles City** — no 2hr bus from Manila needed
- **BKK → CRK is $104-157** (Scoot/Cebu Pacific) — cheap Asia hop
- **CRK → BKK is $373** — direction matters, $216 difference!
- **CVG requires 1-2 stops** for all Philippines/Thailand routes

- **Fly Tue-Thu** for 15-30% savings vs weekends

## Weather (September)

- **Rainy season** — Southwest Monsoon (Habagat)
- **Rainfall:** 200-400mm/month, frequent afternoon/evening downpours
- **Temperature:** 25-32°C (77-90°F), 80-85% humidity
- **Typhoon risk:** Peak season — monitor forecasts
- **Tip:** Pack waterproof gear, plan indoor activities (museums, malls)
- **Silver lining:** Rains usually intense but brief (1-2 hours), mornings often clear

- **Pampanga is the culinary capital of the Philippines** — prioritize food tourism
- **Fly into Clark (CRK)** not Manila — literally in Angeles City
- **Clark Freeport Zone** has good hotels, malls, and restaurants
- **Budget carriers:** Cebu Pacific and Scoot for SEA hops

- **Cebu Pacific Piso Fare sales** happen monthly — follow their Facebook page
- **Visa (US citizens):** 30-day visa-free for both countries, just need valid passport
