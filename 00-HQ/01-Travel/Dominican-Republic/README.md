## On-the-Ground

- **Sosua:** Small beach town, ~25 min from PUJ airport
- **Budget:** $50-100/day (hotel + food + activities)
- **Beach:** Sosua Beach is the main spot, calm water great for swimming
- **Nightlife:** Low-key, beach bars and restaurants
- **Activities:** Snorkeling, diving, whale watching (seasonal), 27 Waterfalls
- **Currency:** Dominican Peso (DOP), ~58 DOP per $1 USD

## Notes

- PUJ (Puerto Plata) is the closest airport to Sosua
- Alternative: Santiago (STI) is larger but 1.5hr drive
- May is start of wet season but still very warm (28-32°C)
- American has best single-stop options (~5hrs)
- Frontier is cheapest but longer travel times (2 stops)
