## 🌧️ Weather & Conditions by Month

### June
- **Start of wet season** — afternoon showers, still mostly sunny mornings
- **Rainfall:** Moderate (~15-20 rainy days/month)
- **Temp:** 27-32°C (80-90°F), humid
- **Typhoon risk:** Low-Moderate
- **Crowds:** Moderate (school's out domestically)

### July
- **Peak wet season** — heavier rain, more overcast
- **Rainfall:** Heavy (~18-22 rainy days/month)
- **Temp:** 26-31°C (79-88°F)
- **Typhoon risk:** Moderate
- **Crowds:** Moderate-High (Western summer holiday)
- **Prices:** Highest of the 4 months

### August
- **Peak wet season continues** — heavy afternoon/evening downpours
- **Rainfall:** Heavy (~18-22 rainy days/month)
- **Temp:** 26-31°C (79-88°F)
- **Typhoon risk:** Moderate-High (peak typhoon season)
- **Crowds:** Moderate
- **Prices:** Good value, dropping from July

### September ⭐ RECOMMENDED
- **Tail end of wet season** — rains starting to ease
- **Rainfall:** Moderate-Heavy (~15-18 rainy days/month)
- **Temp:** 26-31°C (79-88°F)
- **Typhoon risk:** Moderate (but storms less intense)
- **Crowds:** Low (off-peak, fewer tourists)
- **Prices:** Lowest of all months (flights + hotels)

## 🎯 Recommendation

**Go in September.** Here's why:

1. **Cheapest flights** — $799 from SFO, $869 from LAX
2. **Weakest peso** — your USD stretches furthest
3. **Fewer crowds** — more authentic experience, better hotel deals
4. **Weather improving** — rains tapering off, still warm
5. **Best value overall** — 30-45% cheaper than June/July

### Risk: Typhoon Season
- September is technically still typhoon season, but:
  - Most severe typhoons hit October-November
  - You can monitor weather closer to the date
  - Travel insurance recommended (~$50-80)

### If Budget Allows
- Book a flexible fare so you can shift dates if a storm approaches
- Consider travel insurance with weather-related cancellation coverage
