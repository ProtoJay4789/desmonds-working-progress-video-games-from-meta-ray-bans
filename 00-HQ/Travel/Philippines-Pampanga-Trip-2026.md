**Date:** April 28, 2026

## Weather Summary

Pampanga/Angeles City is on the western side of Luzon (similar climate to Manila):
- **June**: Start of heavy rains. Highs ~91°F (33°C), lows ~78°F (26°C)
- **July**: Full rainy season. Highs ~89°F (32°C), lows ~77°F (25°C)
- **August**: Very rainy. Highs ~87°F (30°C), lows ~77°F (25°C)
- **September**: Peak typhoon season. Highs ~88°F (31°C), lows ~77°F (25°C)

**Key considerations:**
- Rainy season (June–November) — expect daily downpours and thunderstorms
- Typhoon risk increases August–November; September is peak
- High humidity makes heat feel worse than temperatures suggest
- Best weather overall: December–February (dry, cooler)

## Price Insights

- **June–September is off-peak** = lower hotel and flight prices
- Peak season (Dec–May) sees 30–50% higher prices
- Clark International Airport (CRK) is the closest airport to Angeles City
- Manila (MNL) is ~2 hours south as alternative

## Recommendation

If budget is primary concern: **June** offers the best balance — off-peak prices, rains just starting, lower typhoon risk than Aug/Sep.

If weather matters more: Consider **November–February** instead.

## Birthday Trip Strategy — 2 Countries (Sep 2026)

### The Big Insight: Open-Jaw Multi-City
Jordan wants Bangkok + Angeles City. The **open-jaw ticket** is the move:
- Fly INTO Bangkok (BKK)
- Fly OUT of Manila (MNL)
- Connect the two with a cheap budget flight

### Pricing Breakdown (Sep 2026, Google Flights data)

| Route | Price | Airline |
|-------|-------|---------|
| LAX → BKK (one-way) | $439 | Cathay Pacific (1 stop HKG) |
| MNL → LAX (one-way) | $473 | Korean Air (1 stop ICN) |
| **Multi-city open-jaw** | **$821** | **Cathay Pacific** |
| BKK → MNL (one-way) | $92 | Scoot (1 stop SIN) |
| **TOTAL** | **~$913** | |

### Why This Works
- Open-jaw priced same as round trip ($821) — no penalty
- BKK→MNL on Scoot/AirAsia is dirt cheap ($92-132)
- Avoids the expensive one-way back from PH to US ($473-522)
- Gets Bangkok AND Angeles City

### Suggested Itinerary
1. LAX → BKK (Cathay Pacific, 1 stop HKG)
2. 3-4 days Bangkok
3. BKK → MNL/CRK (Scoot or AirAsia, ~$92)
4. Bus to Angeles City (~$10)
5. 10-12 days Pampanga
6. MNL → LAX (Korean Air, 1 stop ICN)

### Total Estimated Cost
- Flights: ~$913
- Bus MNL↔Angeles: ~$20
- **Grand total flights+transport: ~$933**
